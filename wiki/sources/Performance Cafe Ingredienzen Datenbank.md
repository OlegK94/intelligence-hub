## Category A — Stimulantien

### Koffein Anhydrous
- Adenosin-Rezeptor-Antagonist (A1/A2A); Autophagie-Induktion (schwach), AMPK-Aktivierung
- Dose: 3–6 mg/kg KG; typical 150–200 mg in coffee context
- EU: ✅ (E 1272; EFSA limit 400 mg/day adults); Heat: 🔥 (sublimiert >178°C)
- COGS: ~0.002 €/portion; Blueprint: ❌ (Bryan Johnson removed 2022)
- Robusta ~2.7% caffeine vs. Arabica ~1.5%

### Guarana-Extrakt (22% Koffein)
- Slower caffeine release via tannin binding; antioxidant synergy via procyanidins
- Dose: 75–300 mg extract; EU: ✅; Heat: 🔥; COGS: ~0.005–0.008 €/portion
- Blueprint: ❌; More of a marketing "natural" caffeine source than functionally superior

### Theacrine (Teacrine, 40%)
- No tolerance development (key advantage vs. caffeine); Dopamin-Agonismus
- Dose: 50–200 mg; Heat: 🔥; COGS: ~0.015–0.030 €/portion
- EU: ⚠️ Grey zone — no explicit Novel Food status, no long consumption history; **legal uncertainty**
- Blueprint: ❌

### Theobromin
- Mild stimulation + bronchodilation + blood pressure lowering; NO release
- Dose: 200–400 mg; natural in dark chocolate (~250 mg/100g)
- EU: ✅; Heat: 🔥 (melting point 357°C); COGS: ~0.006–0.010 €/portion
- Blueprint: ⚠️ unclear (Bryan Johnson consumes cacao-based products)
- **Synergy:** Caffeine + Theobromine naturally in cacao → "Coffee + Cacao" positioning

### Methylliberine (Dynamine 40%)
- Fast energy onset (~15 min); Dopamin/Noradrenalin release
- EU: ⚠️ **Very uncertain — HIGH regulatory risk for EU market** → **NOT recommended for Performance Café**
- COGS: ~0.06–0.09 €/portion (expensive)

## Category B — Nootropika

### L-Theanin
- Alpha-wave induction; GABA agonist; smooths caffeine peaks
- Dose: 100–200 mg; **always combine with caffeine**
- EU: ✅ Novel Food approved (EU 2017/2470, Camellia sinensis extract)
- Heat: 🔥 (amino acid, stable to 250°C); COGS: ~0.006–0.012 €/portion
- **Koffein:Theanin Ratio:**
  - 1:2 → strongest anxiety reduction, max alpha waves → **SKU 1 "Morning Performance"**
  - 2:1 → stronger stimulation → **SKU 2 "Pre-Workout"**

### Alpha-GPC (50% form recommended for sachets)
- ACh precursor; BBB-permeable; GH release via GHRH
- Dose: 300–600 mg/day; Heat: 🌡️ (hygroscopic in humid heat; 50% form more stable)
- EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.014–0.020 €/portion (50% form)
- **50% vs 99% form:** 50% (silica carrier) preferred for sachets — cheaper, more stable, flowable

### Citicoline (CDP-Choline)
- Dual mechanism: ACh precursor AND Cytidin → Uridin → phosphatidylcholine for membrane synthesis
- Dose: 250–500 mg/day; Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.015–0.024 €/portion
- **vs Alpha-GPC:** Alpha-GPC = acute ACh peak (sport); Citicoline = long-term neuroprotection → **Citicoline for SKU 3 Longevity Daily**

### Phosphatidylserin (PS)
- HPA axis modulation → cortisol dampening; EU Health Claim ≥100 mg
- Dose: 300–400 mg/day; Heat: ⚠️ oxidation-sensitive; ideally cold storage
- **Sensory issue:** Waxy/oily texture in hot coffee → emulsified form or capsule recommended
- COGS: ~0.024–0.045 €/portion

### Bacopa Monnieri (45% Bacosides)
- Memory consolidation; BDNF increase; onset only after 4–8 weeks
- Dose: 300–600 mg; Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅ (Ayurvedic, traditional)
- **Key limitation:** Fat required for bacoside absorption — problematic in black coffee; **capsule or coconut oil addition recommended**

## Category D — Funktionspilze

### Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus, 8:1 Dual Extract)
- NGF synthesis (Hericenone/Erinacin); myelin regeneration; BDNF increase (indirect)
- Dose: 500–1000 mg; **dual extract mandatory** (water = beta-glucans; alcohol = hericenones — water-only misses active terpenes)
- Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.060–0.100 €/portion
- Blueprint: ✅ (Super Shrooms)

### Cordyceps Militaris (>25% Cordycepin + >25% Beta-Glucane)
- VO2max +11% (1680 mg/day, 3 weeks, elderly); ATP production via adenosine receptor activation
- Use Cordyceps militaris (cultured, EU-approved) NOT Cordyceps sinensis (wild, unavailable)
- Dose: 1000–3000 mg; Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.100–0.180 €/portion
- Blueprint: ✅ (Super Shrooms)

### Reishi (Ganoderma lucidum, Dual Extract)
- Ganoderic Acids → autophagy (mTOR inhibition); NK-cell activation
- Dose: 1500–3000 mg/day; **1000 mg abends** for sleep
- Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.120–0.225 €/portion (most expensive mushroom per portion)
- Blueprint: ✅ (Super Shrooms)

### Chaga (Inonotus obliquus)
- Highest ORAC value of all mushrooms; Betulinic Acid → apoptosis (tumor cells); Melanin complex as superoxide scavenger
- Dose: 1000–2000 mg; Heat: 🔥 (traditionally brewed as hot tea)
- EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.060–0.100 €/portion; Blueprint: ✅ (Super Shrooms)

### Turkey Tail (Trametes versicolor, >30% PSK/PSP)
- PSK (Krestin) approved in Japan for cancer adjuvant therapy; prebiotic for Bifidobacterium/Lactobacillus
- Dose: 1500–3000 mg; Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.075–0.120 €/portion
- Blueprint: ✅ (Super Shrooms)

## Category E — Aminosäuren

### L-Tyrosin vs. NALT

| Criterion | L-Tyrosin | NALT |
|---|---|---|
| Bioavailability | ~54% oral | ~18% (acetyl group hinders conversion) |
| Optimal dose | 500–2000 mg | 300–1200 mg |
| Bulk price/kg | ~15–25 € | ~40–70 € |
| Verdict | **Use this** | Marketing product — inferior |

### Taurin ⭐ (Science 2023 Longevity Key Finding)
- **Yadav et al. Science 2023:** Taurine deficiency = driver of aging; +12% lifespan in mice; blood taurine drops to 1/3 of youth level by age 30
- Longevity pathways: mitochondrial tRNA stabilization; Ca²⁺ buffering; antioxidant
- Dose: 1000–3000 mg/day (longevity: 6 g/day in mice; human equivalent ~40 mg/kg)
- Heat: 🔥 (aminosulfonic acid, very stable); EU: ✅ (in energy drinks); COGS: ~0.008–0.015 €/portion
- Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson 2g/day, Blueprint 2026 stack)

### L-Citrullin Malat (2:1)
- NO production → vasodilation; ammonia buffering in urea cycle
- Dose: 6–8 g L-Citrullin or 8 g Citrullin-Malat 2:1 (pre-workout)
- Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.090–0.150 €/portion
- **Sensory bonus:** Slightly sour taste — compatible with coffee

### Beta-Alanin
- Carnosine synthesis → intramuscular H⁺ buffer; +2-3% endurance in 1–4 min high-intensity efforts
- **Problem:** Paresthesia (tingling) at >800 mg/dose — bad UX in premium coffee
- **Only in SKU 2 as CarnoSyn SR (sustained release form)** to minimize tingling
- Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.019–0.032 €/portion (1600 mg)

### Kreatin Monohydrat ⚠️ Heat-Critical
- 3–5 g/day; ATP resynthesis; cognitive function (increasingly evidenced)
- **Heat stability CRITICAL:** Stable as dry powder at 90°C, BUT dissolved in hot acidic coffee (pH ~5) → converts to creatinine (biologically inactive); rate increases with temperature + acidity; relevant loss if >10 min standing
- **Solution:** Include in stick-pack as dry powder with instruction "add and drink immediately" OR as separate capsule
- EU: ✅ (EFSA: safe to 3 g/day general population); Blueprint: ✅ (Blueprint Creatine 2.5 g/day)
- COGS: ~0.030–0.054 €/portion

## Category F — Longevity Core (Regulatory Critical)

### NAC (N-Acetyl-Cystein)
- Glutathione precursor (master antioxidant); Nrf2 activation; NF-κB inhibition
- Dose: 600–1200 mg/day; Blueprint: ✅ (Blueprint NAC+Ginger+Curcumin, 600 mg)
- Heat: 🌡️ (thiol group oxidation-sensitive in 90°C aqueous solution; protect with inert gas sealing)
- **EU Status 2024 (COMPLEX):**
  - NOT Novel Food — not in EU Novel Food Catalogue
  - Available as NEM in Germany/EU — legally sold, not explicitly banned
  - Also licensed as Arzneimittel (Fluimucil, NAC AL 600) → dual status
  - EU handles NEM/pharma dual status differently from US FDA (which banned NAC as supplement)
  - BfR: No active warning against NAC as NEM
  - **Risk:** Doses >1200 mg may be classified as drug-like → use 600 mg; no therapeutic claims
  - **Sensory problem:** Sulfur odor (thiol group) → requires flavorist masking
  - **Action:** German food law attorney before market entry

### Curcumin (liposomal/mizellar)
- NF-κB inhibition → TNF-α, IL-6, IL-1β reduction; Nrf2 activation; autophagy
- Standard + Piperin: +2000% bioavailability; Liposomal: 200–500 mg; Mizellar: 50–100 mg effective
- Heat: ⚠️ (hydrophobic, light-sensitive; micellar improves solubility but not heat stability)
- EU: ✅ (E100 food color + NEM); COGS: ~0.030–0.060 €/portion liposomal
- Blueprint: ✅ (Blueprint NAC+Ginger+Curcumin)

### Quercetin
- Senolytic (BCL-2/XL inhibitor → apoptosis in senescent cells); AMPK activation; zinc ionophore
- Dose: Senolytic 500–1000 mg intermittent; daily 250–500 mg
- Heat: 🌡️ (~20–30% loss at 90°C aqueous); Phytosom form more stable
- EU: ✅ from Sophora japonica (traditional source) — ⚠️ Dimorphandra mollis source = Novel Food!
- COGS: ~0.015–0.030 €/portion

### Fisetin ⚠️ Regulatory Grey Zone
- **Potenteste natürliches Senolytikum** (in vitro + in vivo > Quercetin)
- BDNF increase; PI3K/AKT/mTOR inhibition; BCL-2/BCL-XL inhibition
- Dose: Senolytic 100–500 mg intermittent; daily 100 mg
- Heat: 🌡️ (heat- and oxidation-sensitive; liposomal form recommended)
- EU: ⚠️ **Grey zone — de facto NEM, legally uncertain; BfR consultation recommended**
- COGS: ~0.010–0.020 €/portion (100 mg)

### Urolithin A ❌ EU BLOCKER
- Mitophagy via PINK1/Parkin pathway; PGC-1α → mitochondrial biogenesis
- Dose: 500–1000 mg/day (Amazentis RCT: 500 mg significant effects)
- Heat: 🔥; COGS: ~0.40–0.60 €/portion (high)
- EU: ❌ **Novel Food NOT APPROVED (as of June 2026)** — Amazentis application 2018-0538, EFSA pending; **earliest realistic approval 2027/2028**
- Patent: ⚠️ Amazentis/Timeline >100 patents; generic from China = IP violation risk
- **BLOCKER:** Cannot use in EU product. **Alternative: pomegranate extract (ellagic acid → body produces own Urolithin A) — no patent/Novel Food issues**
- Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson uses Mitopure by Timeline)

### Spermidine ✅ EU-APPROVED LONGEVITY WINNER
- Autophagy via eIF5A hypusination; epigenetic modification (histone acetylation); mTOR inhibition
- Dose: 1–5 mg Spermidine/day; 1 g wheat germ extract (at 5 mg/g content)
- Heat: 🌡️ (polyamines can oxidize at high heat; short-term stable)
- **EU: ✅ APPROVED as Novel Food — EU Regulation 2020/443 (25 March 2020)** for spermidine-rich wheat germ extract (Triticum aestivum); max 6 mg Spermidine/day
- COGS: ~0.075–0.150 €/portion (1.5 g extract for 3 mg Spermidine)
- Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson 2.4 mg Spermidine/day, wheat germ extract)
- **Strategic note:** "Strongest available longevity compound with confirmed EU status in this SKU"

### NMN ⚠️ EU PENDING
- NAD⁺ precursor; Sirtuin activation (SIRT1-7); PARP DNA repair; Erasure of epigenetic aging
- Dose: 250–1000 mg/day; sublingual potentially more effective
- Heat: ⚠️ **UNSTABLE — oxidation-sensitive; degrades at 90°C in solution; stable as dry powder to ~60°C only. Inert gas sealing mandatory; NOT in hot coffee!**
- EU: ⚠️ **Novel Food IN REVIEW (June 2026):** EFSA issued positive safety opinion for EffePharm NMN (NutraIngredients, May 2026); additional Chinese applications in review (PC-1537); formal approval expected **2026/2027**; currently NOT legally marketable as NEM in EU
- COGS: ~0.100–0.200 €/portion (500 mg); EU-supplier Longevir (FSSC 22000 certified)
- Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson 500 mg NMN/day)
- **BLOCKER:** Not yet approved in EU. Alternative: NR (some markets) or Niacin as NAD+ precursor

### NR (Nicotinamid-Ribosid) ⚠️ Chromadex Patent
- NAD⁺ via NRK1/2 kinases; different absorption pathway than NMN
- Heat: ⚠️ (oxidation- and heat-sensitive like NMN; inert gas recommended)
- EU: ⚠️ **Grey zone — not officially Novel Food-approved; inconsistently sold in EU markets**
- Patent: ⚠️ **Chromadex (NIAGEN) holds extensive NR patents; generic from China = IP violation risk**
- Blueprint: ❌ (Bryan Johnson prefers NMN over NR)

### Resveratrol ⚠️ Bryan Johnson Controversy
- SIRT1 activation; AMPK; caloric restriction mimetic
- Dose: 150–500 mg/day with fat; Heat: ⚠️ (isomerizes trans→cis at heat/UV → inactive)
- EU: ✅ (NEM, Fallopia japonica extract); COGS: ~0.012–0.025 €/portion
- **Blueprint status CONTROVERSIAL:** Bryan Johnson removed Resveratrol from daily stack. Reason: 2023/2024 meta-analyses show Resveratrol + endurance training **inhibits training-induced mitochondrial biogenesis** (as antioxidant it blunts the training stimulus). Johnson now takes situationally only.

### Astaxanthin
- Strongest natural antioxidant (6000× vitamin C); crosses cell membranes; mtROS reduction
- Dose: 4–12 mg/day; Heat: ⚠️ (carotenoid; oxidation-sensitive; loses color and activity at 90°C)
- EU: ✅ (Haematococcus pluvialis extract as NEM since 2014); COGS: ~0.004–0.008 €/portion (extremely cheap per portion despite high $/kg)
- Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson takes Astaxanthin daily)
- **Note:** Emulsion form more stable; not ideal for hot coffee without protection

### CoQ10 / Ubiquinol
- Mitochondrial electron transport (Complex I/II/III); membrane antioxidant
- Dose: Ubiquinol 100–200 mg/day; Ubiquinon 200–400 mg/day
- Heat: ⚠️; **Solubility CRITICAL: strongly hydrophobic — forms oil droplets in coffee without emulsifier — sensorially unacceptable**
- EU: ✅; Blueprint: ✅ (Bryan Johnson daily)
- **Recommendation:** Emulsified form (Kaneka QH micro-crystals, micellar CoQ10) or separate softgel capsule — **NOT directly in hot coffee**

### Trigonellin ⭐ Marketing Differentiator
- **2024 Nature Aging study:** Trigonellin raises muscular NAD⁺ in older subjects — NMN-like effect!
- Natural coffee constituent: Light Roast ~1 g/100g Trigonellin (→ Niacin → NAD⁺)
- Heat: 🌡️ (converts to Niacin during roasting; Light Roast preserves more than Dark Roast)
- EU: ✅ (natural coffee ingredient; no Novel Food question)
- **Strategic message: "Our Light Roast coffee is already a NAD⁺ booster — before we add a single ingredient."**

## Category G — Vitamine & Mineralien

### Magnesium Glycinat
- 300+ enzymatic reactions; NMDA antagonist (anxiolytic, sleep-promoting); Glycine synergistic
- Dose: 200–400 mg elemental Mg/day (= 1400–2800 mg Mg Glycinate at 14% Mg content)
- Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.084–0.168 €/portion; Blueprint: ✅

### Magnesium L-Threonat (Magtein — patented)
- **Crosses BBB** (patented by MIT/Magtein brand); +15% hippocampal synapses (MIT study)
- Only ~7-8% Mg content vs. 14% for Glycinate
- EU: ✅; COGS: ~0.060–0.120 €/portion
- **Recommendation:** Mg Glycinate for SKU 3 Longevity (sleep, general); Mg L-Threonat for SKU 1 Morning Performance (cognitive + differentiation point)

### Vitamin D3 + K2 ⚠️ Fat-Solubility in Coffee
- D3: VDR activation → >1000 gene expressions; K2 MK-7: calcium routing (Osteocalcin + Matrix-GLA-protein)
- Dose: D3 2000–5000 IU/day; K2 100–200 mcg/day
- Heat: ⚠️ **Fat-soluble vitamins — insoluble in hot coffee without fat; bioavailability near zero**
- Solutions: (1) Emulsified D3/K2 in MCT/lecithin; (2) Beta-cyclodextrin complex; (3) Separate capsule
- EU: ✅; Blueprint: ✅; COGS: ~0.003–0.008 €/portion (very cheap)

### B-Komplex (B6, B12, Folsäure)
- B6 (P5P): Homocysteine metabolism; neurotransmitter synthesis — Heat: 🔥
- B12 (Methylcobalamin): Methylation; myelin — Heat: 🌡️ (light-sensitive)
- Folsäure/Methylfolat: One-carbon metabolism; DNA methylation — Heat: 🌡️ (partially unstable at high temp)
- EU: ✅ all; Blueprint: ✅

### Zink + Selen
- Zink Glyzinat 15–25 mg: immune, testosterone, DNA repair — Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅
- Selenomethionin 100–200 mcg: glutathione peroxidase, thyroid — Heat: 🔥; EU: ✅ (safety limit 300 mcg/day)
- Blueprint: ✅ both

### Omega-3 (EPA + DHA) ❌ Not in Hot Coffee
- Anti-inflammatory (SPM pathway); DHA = structural brain lipid; 1000–3000 mg EPA+DHA/day
- Heat: ❌ **CANNOT USE IN HOT COFFEE — oxidizes strongly → trans fatty acids, rancidity; sensorially catastrophic; even emulsified forms unstable at 90°C**
- **Recommendation: Separate capsule or RTD cold-addition format only**
- EU: ✅; Blueprint: ✅
