## A) Robusta vs. Arabica — Key Comparison

### Core Comparison Table

| Parameter | Robusta (*C. canephora*) | Arabica (*C. arabica*) |
|---|---|---|
| Koffein (% TG) | 2.0–4.5% | 1.1–1.7% |
| Chlorogensäure (% TG Rohbohne) | **7–12%** | 4–8% |
| Chlorogensäure (mg/100ml Espresso) | 150–300 mg | 80–180 mg |
| Trigonellin (mg/100g Rohbohne) | 600–900 mg | **900–1,200 mg** |
| Lipide gesamt (% TG) | 8–12% | 14–18% |
| Cafestol/Kahweol | Cafestol vorhanden, Kahweol minimal | Beide signifikant (2–5× mehr als Robusta) |
| Preis EU-Großhandel 2025 | **1.50–3.50 €/kg** | 3.00–8.00 €/kg |
| Longevity-Relevanz Score | **8/10** | 6/10 |

### Why Robusta for Longevity Coffee

1. **Chlorogensäure:** Robusta contains up to **2× more CGA than Arabica** (7–12% vs. 4–8% TG). CGA activates AMPK (same pathway as Metformin), improves insulin sensitivity, has antioxidative and anti-inflammatory effects. Primary longevity bioactive in coffee.

2. **Koffein:** Double the caffeine per gram at same dose → more space in sachet for functional ingredients; lower coffee quantity needed for Pre-Workout target (150–200 mg).

3. **Preis-Wirkstoff-Verhältnis:** 50–70% cheaper than Arabica, but more CGA and caffeine per kg.

4. **Einschränkung Trigonellin:** Arabica contains **more Trigonellin** (900–1,200 mg/100g vs. 600–900 mg/100g). Since Trigonellin was identified as natural NAD⁺ precursor (Yoshino et al., *Cell Metabolism* 2024), this favors a **mixed strategy**: Robusta for CGA + caffeine, Arabica for Trigonellin.

## B) Vietnamese Robusta — Buôn Ma Thuột / Dak Lak

### Geographic Profile

- **Province:** Dak Lak, Central Highlands Vietnam
- **Altitude:** 500–800 m (S18 parcels to 800 m)
- **Soil:** Red basalt ("Terra Roxa") — mineral-rich, high water retention
- **Climate:** Tropical, 18–28°C year-round, ~1,800 mm annual rainfall
- **City:** Buôn Ma Thuột — "Coffee Capital of Vietnam"

### Koffein by Grade

| Herkunft | Koffein % TG |
|---|---|
| Standard Vietnam Robusta | 1.5–2.0% |
| Dak Lak / Buôn Ma Thuột | 2.0–2.5% |
| **Dak Lak S18 Premium** | **2.3–2.7%** |
| Arabica (Referenz) | 1.1–1.7% |

### Recommended Grade: S18 Washed Dak Lak

- Siebgröße 18/64 Zoll (≈ 7.14 mm) — denser, heavier beans
- Washed processing: cleaner profile, consistent flavor, preferred for functional products
- Higher CGA content, optimal for Instant processing

## C) Blend Ratios — 5 Scenarios

**Berechnungsgrundlage:** Robusta 2.5% Koffein, 9% CGA, 750 mg/100g Trigonellin; Arabica 1.4% Koffein, 5.5% CGA, 1,050 mg/100g Trigonellin; 2g portion

| Verhältnis R/A | Koffein/2g | CGA/2g | Trigonellin/2g | Longevity Score |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 100/0 | ~50 mg | ~180 mg | ~15 mg | 7/10 |
| 80/20 | ~45 mg | ~155 mg | ~17 mg | 8/10 |
| **60/40** | **~40 mg** | **~136 mg** | **~20 mg** | **9/10** |
| 40/60 | ~35 mg | ~118 mg | ~23 mg | 7/10 |
| 20/80 | ~30 mg | ~99 mg | ~26 mg | 5/10 |

**Recommendation: 60/40 Robusta/Arabica** — maximizes CGA, sufficient caffeine, Arabica provides Trigonellin + aroma balance.

### Korrekturfaktoren nach Röstung + Instant-Extraktion

- Koffein: ×0.95 (stabil)
- CGA: ×0.45–0.55 (Light Roast); ×0.35–0.45 (Medium Roast)
- Trigonellin: ×0.15–0.35 (Light Roast erhält mehr)

## D) Röstgrad — Longevity-Optimized

| Parameter | Light Roast (210–220°C) | Medium Roast (220–230°C) | Dark Roast (230–250°C+) |
|---|---|---|---|
| CGA-Erhalt | **45–55%** | 35–45% | <15% |
| Trigonellin-Erhalt | **30–40%** | 10–25% | <10% |
| Koffein-Erhalt | ~95–100% | ~92–98% | ~88–95% |
| Acrylamid (µg/kg) | 93–200 | 150–300 (vor Abbau) | <100 (nach Abbau) |

**Recommendation: City Roast / Light-Medium (Medium-Light)**
- Zieltemperatur: **215–218°C Innentemperatur**
- Development Time: 1:45–2:00 min nach First Crack
- Agtron: ~65
- Begründung: maximaler CGA-Erhalt + Trigonellin-Erhalt + akzeptabler Geschmack + Pilzextrakt-Kompatibilität

## E) Instant-Extraktion — Method Comparison

| Methode | CGA-Erhalt | Trigonellin | Koffein | Kosten €/kg | EU-CMO-Verfügbarkeit | Empfehlung |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Spray-Dried | 50–65% | 60–70% | 85–92% | 8–15 € | Sehr hoch | Nein |
| **Freeze-Dried** | **70–85%** | **75–85%** | **90–95%** | **15–25 €** | **Hoch** | **JA — Phase 1** |
| Spray-Freeze-Dried | 80–85% | ~85% | 90–95% | 20–35 € | Niedrig | Option Phase 2 |
| Cold Brew gefriergetrocknet | 80–90% | 85–90% | 88–95% | 30–50 € | Sehr niedrig | Nein Phase 1 |

**Freeze-Dried is recommended for Phase 1:** best balance of bioactive retention, taste, cost, and EU CMO availability.

## F) Finale Blend-Empfehlung

**Kaffeebasis:**
> **60% Vietnam Robusta S18 Washed (Dak Lak) + 40% Äthiopien Arabica Washed (Yirgacheffe oder Sidama)**

**Röstgrad:** City Roast, 215–218°C, Agtron ~65

**Extraktion:** Freeze-Dried Instant Coffee, 85–90°C, ~30–40% TDS

**Longevity-Wirkstoffgehalt pro 2g Sachet (nach Freeze-Drying):**

| Wirkstoff | Menge/2g Portion | Klinisch relevante Tagesdosis |
|---|---|---|
| Chlorogensäure | 25–45 mg | 200–400 mg/Tag |
| Trigonellin | 3–6 mg | Keine etablierte Referenz |
| Koffein | 29–34 mg | 150–200 mg Pre-Workout |

> **Koffein-Empfehlung:** 100–150 mg synthetisches Koffein USP hinzufügen → Gesamtkoffein 130–185 mg/Portion.

**Geschmacksprofil:** Schokolade, dunkle Kirsche, Mandel (Robusta) + Zitrus-Touch, florale Anklänge (Äthiopien Arabica)
