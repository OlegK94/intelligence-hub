### GF-Haftung Note

German law (§ 15a InsO) requires a GmbH/UG Geschäftsführer to file for insolvency within **21 days** of the company becoming insolvent (Zahlungsunfähigkeit) or over-indebted (Überschuldung). Failure to file is a **criminal offense** (Insolvenzverschleppung). With Wagglz GmbH carrying a 5.000 € Jahresabschluss liability and potentially other obligations, the insolvency risk flag is serious and requires immediate document review.
