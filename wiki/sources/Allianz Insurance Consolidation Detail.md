## Decision

**3 new policies** to be switched to Allianz → 5-policy threshold reached → 20% discount unlocked.

### Why Risikolebensversicherung Stays

The term life policy is explicitly excluded from the consolidation:

1. **New health assessment required** — switching triggers a fresh Gesundheitsprüfung
2. **Higher entry age** — new contract would use current (older) age as entry age → permanently higher premiums
3. **Discount does not justify the risk** — the 20% savings on this policy do not outweigh the potential premium increase

This is a sound actuarial decision: term life premiums are locked at the entry-age rate. Re-entering at a higher age creates a permanent cost disadvantage that cannot be reversed.
