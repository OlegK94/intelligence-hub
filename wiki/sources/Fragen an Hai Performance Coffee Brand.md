# Fragen an Hai — Performance Coffee Brand (Source Detail)

## Overview

This source document (status: Offen, date: 2026-06-15) is a structured due-diligence questionnaire prepared by [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]] for potential co-founder [[Hai]] in the context of the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] venture. All items are open (unchecked), indicating the conversation has not yet taken place as of the document date.

> For the Performance Coffee Brand project overview, see [[Performance Coffee Brand]].
> For the entity page on Hai as co-founder, see [[Hai]].

## Themenblöcke (Topic Areas)

### 1. Produkt & Know-How

- Was ist dein fachlicher Hintergrund? (Lebensmitteltechnologie, Pharma, Marketing, Sales?)
- Hast du Erfahrung mit Supplement-/Funktionslebensmittel-Produktentwicklung?
- Kennst du CMOs oder Produktionspartner in DACH/EU?
- Hast du bereits Kontakt zu Laboren oder Regulatory-Affairs-Beratern?
- Was ist deine Einschätzung zu [[NMN]] im EU-Markt — hast du da schon recherchiert?

**Significance:** These questions probe whether Hai brings the **technical and regulatory expertise** that Oleg's existing research identifies as critical blockers — specifically the [[EU Novel Food Regulation]] for NMN, lab relationships, and CMO access. If Hai has these connections, it would significantly accelerate Phase 1.

### 2. Netzwerk

- Welche konkreten Kontakte bringst du mit? (Investoren, Gyms, CrossFit, Hyrox, B2B?)
- Hast du Zugang zur Biohacker-Community ([[Bryan Johnson]]-Follower, Longevity-Netzwerke)?
- Kennst du potenzielle Angel-Investoren in DACH?

**Significance:** The target customer segments for [[Performance Coffee Brand]] are Hyrox athletes, CrossFit community, and biohackers (Blueprint/Bryan Johnson followers). Hai's network access to these communities is a direct commercial asset. Angel investor access in DACH unlocks Phase 2 scaling.

### 3. Operative Rolle

- Welche Bereiche willst du operativ leiten? (Produktion, Vertrieb, Marketing, Ops?)
- Wie viel Zeit kannst du pro Woche investieren?
- Hast du noch andere berufliche Verpflichtungen aktuell?

**Significance:** Complements the parallel questions in [[Fragen an Hai Café Berlin Partnership]] (if that document exists). The time commitment question mirrors the cafe partnership structure questions in [[Café Berlin Partnership Hai — Planning Detail]]. This is a vetting question to assess whether Hai can be a full co-founder or a more limited partner.

### 4. Kapital

- Welches Budget kannst du für Phase 0–1 einbringen? (~5.000–8.000 € gesamt)
- Bist du bereit die UG-Gründungskosten 50/50 zu teilen?
- Wie siehst du die Finanzierung bei späterer Skalierung (Bootstrapped vs. Funding)?

**Significance:** Phase 0–1 budget of **5.000–8.000 €** aligns with the minimal viable product (MVP) approach documented in [[Performance Cafe README Project Setup]]. The UG-Gründungskosten reference confirms that a **new UG** is the planned legal entity (not Oleg's existing GmbH) — a structurally significant decision. See [[Oleg Business Entity Structure]].

> ⚠️ **Note on Legal Entity:** The question "Bist du bereit die UG-Gründungskosten 50/50 zu teilen?" implies the Performance Coffee Brand will be incorporated as a **new UG**, distinct from Oleg's existing entities. This contradicts the earlier [[Café Berlin Partnership Hai — Planning Detail]] question about using "GmbH (Oleg, ruhend)" as operating vehicle — but those are different ventures. The Performance Coffee Brand appears to be scoped as a separate new UG.

### 5. Gesellschaft & Partnership

- Bist du mit 4 Jahre Vesting / 1 Jahr Cliff einverstanden?
- Wie willst du bei Uneinigkeit entscheiden — Mediator, Buy-Sell-Klausel?
- Wagglz GmbH als operative Einheit — passt dir das oder lieber Neugründung?
- Wo siehst du dich in 3 Jahren mit diesem Business?

**Significance:** The **4-year vesting / 1-year cliff** is standard founder vesting — Oleg is applying Silicon Valley-style co-founder protection. The **Wagglz GmbH** reference is significant: this is [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]]'s existing GmbH (see [[Oleg Business Entity Structure]]), suggesting it may serve as the operating entity rather than a new UG — *apparently in tension with the UG reference in Section 4*.

> ⚠️ **Contradiction:** Section 4 asks about splitting "UG-Gründungskosten" (implying a new UG), while Section 5 asks about "Wagglz GmbH als operative Einheit" (implying using the existing GmbH). These two questions present conflicting entity options — both are open questions, not decisions. The planning process has not resolved which entity to use.

### 6. Produkt-Vision

- Welchen Namen favorisierst du? (**AEVUM** / **KOLVR** / **ORVYN** oder eigene Idee?)
- Wie siehst du die Zielgruppe — eher Athlet oder Executive oder Biohacker?
- Blueprint-Strategie nachahmen (Transparenz, COAs, Community) — einverstanden?
- Wann willst du den ersten Prototypen in der Hand haben?

**Significance:** Three candidate brand names are presented: **AEVUM**, **KOLVR**, **ORVYN**. These appear here for the first time in the wiki and should be captured in the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] entity page. The COA (Certificate of Analysis) transparency strategy mirrors [[Bryan Johnson]]'s Blueprint approach. The prototype timeline question sets expectations for velocity.
