# Andrew Huberman

## Overview

[[Andrew Huberman]] is a Stanford University neuroscientist, tenured professor in the department of neurobiology and ophthalmology, and host of the popular Huberman Lab podcast. He translates cutting-edge neuroscience research into practical, evidence-based protocols for health and performance optimization.

## Source Information

- **Platform:** YouTube (@hubermanlab)
- **Format:** Long-form podcast episodes
- **Focus:** Neuroscience-based health optimization
- **Audience:** General public seeking evidence-based health strategies
