## 1%-Regelung vs. Fahrtenbuch

### 1%-Regelung (Pauschalmethod — current)
- Monthly taxable benefit = **1% × Listenpreis (Bruttolistenpreis)**
- Simple, no documentation required
- Advantageous when private use is high
- **Formula:** 36.400 € × 1% = **364 €/Monat**

### Fahrtenbuch (Actual Cost Method — under review)
- Monthly taxable benefit = **Actual private mileage / Total mileage × Total car costs**
- Requires meticulous daily mileage log for 100% of trips
- More admin-intensive but potentially lower taxable benefit
- **Advantageous when:** Private use is genuinely low relative to business use
