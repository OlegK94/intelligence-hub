# Chlorogensäure (Chlorogenic Acid / CGA)

## Overview

**Chlorogensäure** (Chlorogenic Acid, CGA) is the primary polyphenol in coffee — an ester of caffeic acid and quinic acid. It is the single most important longevity-active compound in coffee for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] stack, activating the same cellular energy-sensing pathway (AMPK) as metformin and exercise.

---

## Chemistry

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Type | Hydroxycinnamic acid ester (caffeic acid + quinic acid) |
| Classification | Polyphenol, chlorogenic acid family |
| Location | Highest in green/unroasted coffee beans |
| Per Cup (Light Roast) | **250–350 mg** |
| Per Cup (Medium Roast) | ~150–250 mg |
| Per Cup (Dark Roast 220°C+) | **50–100 mg** |

---

## Roast Gradient — Critical for Product

CGA is degraded by heat during roasting. This is one of the core reasons [[Performance Coffee Brand]] specifies **Light Roast** as the coffee base:

| Roast | Temperature | CGA per Cup | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light | 150–160°C | 250–350 mg | ✅ **Recommended** |
| Medium | ~175–185°C | 150–250 mg | ✅ Acceptable |
| Dark | 220°C+ | 50–100 mg | ❌ Avoid for CGA content |

## Mechanisms of Action

### 1. AMPK Activation (Primary)
- CGA phosphorylates AMPK via the CAMKK2 pathway
- In hepatocytes: glucose uptake increases 15–30% (HepG2 studies)
- Same pathway as **metformin** and **exercise** → highly credible longevity mechanism
- *Source: Ong KW et al., BBRC 2012*

### 2. Insulin Sensitivity (RCT Evidence)
- RCT (n=56, T2DM risk): 400 mg CGA/day for 12 weeks
- Result: Fasting glucose −8 mg/dL, HOMA-IR −0.4
- *Source: Johnston KL et al., AJCN 2003*

### 3. GLP-1 Potentiation
- CGA increases postprandial GLP-1 secretion
- → Improved satiety and glucose control
- *Source: Zheng G et al., J Agric Food Chem 2004*

### 4. NF-κB Inhibition (Anti-Inflammatory)
- Direct NF-κB inhibition in macrophages
- → Reduces [[Inflammaging]] upstream
- Meta-analyses confirm hs-CRP reduction with regular coffee consumption

### 5. Antioxidant Action
- Scavenges reactive oxygen species (ROS)
- Indirect support for [[Mitochondriale Dysfunktion]] hallmark via oxidative stress reduction
- Indirect support for [[Genomische Instabilität]] via reduced DNA damage

## Synergy in Coffee Stack

```
Chlorogensäure
    ↓
AMPK-Aktivierung + NF-κB-Hemmung
    ↓
Insulinsensitivität ↑ + Inflammaging ↓ + Mitochondriale Biogenese ↑ (PGC-1α)
```

**Synergy with koffein:** Both activate AMPK (via different mechanisms — CGA via CAMKK2, koffein via adenosine receptor blockade). Combined effect is synergistic, not merely additive.

**Synergy with Trigonellin:** CGA addresses AMPK/mTOR axis; trigonellin addresses NAD+/Sirtuin axis — complementary longevity pathways from the same cup.

## Marketing Claims (EFSA-Compatible)

✅ Permissible:
- "Contributes to normal blood glucose regulation" (via AMPK mechanism)
- "Rich in polyphenols" (factual content claim)
- "Contains chlorogenic acid" (compositional claim)

❌ Not permissible under EFSA:
- "Anti-aging"
- "Extends lifespan"
- "Activates longevity pathways" (not an approved health claim)

**Recommended approach:** Biomarker narrative — "Our light-roast base delivers up to 300 mg of chlorogenic acid per serving, supporting hs-CRP and fasting glucose levels within healthy ranges."
