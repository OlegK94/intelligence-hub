# Coffee Blend Ratios — Performance Coffee

## Overview

The Robusta/Arabica blend ratio is a key formulation decision for [[Performance Coffee Brand]]. The two coffee species have complementary strengths: Robusta for [[Chlorogenic Acid Longevity|CGA]] and caffeine, Arabica for [[Trigonellin NAD Precursor|Trigonellin]] and aroma complexity. Five blend scenarios were analyzed.

## Calculation Basis

| Parameter | Robusta (Dak Lak S18) | Arabica (Ethiopia) |
|---|---|---|
| Koffein (% TG) | 2.5% | 1.4% |
| CGA (% TG) | 9% | 5.5% |
| Trigonellin (mg/100g) | 750 mg | 1,050 mg |
| Portion | 2g Kaffeepulver im Sachet | — |

*Note: All values are raw bean basis before roasting. Correction factors applied for processing losses.*

## Five Blend Scenarios

| Verhältnis R/A | Koffein/2g | CGA/2g | Trigonellin/2g | Longevity Score | Geschmack |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 100/0 | ~50 mg | ~180 mg | ~15 mg | 7/10 | Bitter, erdig, wenig Komplexität |
| 80/20 | ~45 mg | ~155 mg | ~17 mg | 8/10 | Stark, leichte Fruchttöne, immer noch bitter |
| **60/40** | **~40 mg** | **~136 mg** | **~20 mg** | **9/10** | **Ausgewogen: Körper + Aroma** |
| 40/60 | ~35 mg | ~118 mg | ~23 mg | 7/10 | Fruchtiger, heller, weniger Körper |
| 20/80 | ~30 mg | ~99 mg | ~26 mg | 5/10 | Nahe reinem Arabica, elegant aber wenig Wirkstoff |

## Recommendation: 60/40 Robusta/Arabica (Longevity Score 9/10)

**Rationale:**
- **Maximizes CGA** (~136 mg/2g raw → ~25–45 mg/sachet after processing) — most important longevity bioactive
- **Sufficient caffeine** (~40 mg/2g raw; after processing ~29–34 mg from coffee → topped up with 100–150 mg synthetic caffeine for 130–185 mg total per sachet)
- **Trigonellin contribution** (~20 mg/2g raw → ~3–6 mg/sachet) from the 40% Arabica component
- **Taste:** Western consumer "sweet spot" — strong body without unacceptable bitterness
- **Functional ingredient masking:** Lion's Mane / Chaga bitterness partially overlaid by Robusta body; Arabica adds brightness

## Final Recommended Blend

> **60% Vietnam Robusta S18 Washed (Dak Lak) + 40% Ethiopian Arabica Washed (Yirgacheffe or Sidama)**

### Why Ethiopian Arabica (not Colombian)

1. **Higher Trigonellin** — Ethiopian heirloom varieties (Coffea arabica var. Bourbon, Typica, Gesha) are genetically more diverse → higher Trigonellin content
2. **Floral-fruity aromatics** — Jasmine, citrus, peach complement Robusta body
3. **Premium positioning** — Ethiopia as origin country of coffee supports brand narrative
4. **Price:** 4–6 €/kg green coffee — affordable for Specialty grade

### Caffeine Strategy

With 2g Freeze-Dried Instant Powder, natural caffeine from the blend is only 29–34 mg — insufficient for Pre-Workout positioning (target: 150–200 mg).

**Solution:** Add **100–150 mg synthetic caffeine USP** (99% pure, ~5 €/kg) → Total caffeine: **130–185 mg/sachet**

This is:
- Commercially standard (used in all energy drinks and most pre-workout products)
- Labeled as "Koffein" — no distinction required between natural and synthetic caffeine in EU food law
- Pre-Workout optimal range (150–200 mg)

## Taste Profile of Final Blend (60/40, City Roast, Freeze-Dried)

- **Base note:** Chocolate, dark cherry, almond (Robusta body)
- **Mid:** Citrus touch, floral hints (Ethiopian Arabica)
- **Finish:** Long, slightly herbaceous, no acidic aftertaste
- **Bitterness:** Moderate (7/10) — ideal for functional additions

## Post-Processing Values (Sachet Reality)

| Wirkstoff | 2g Sachet nach Freeze-Drying | Klinische Referenz |
|---|---|---|
| Chlorogensäure | **25–45 mg** | 200–400 mg/Tag (Multiple Sachets) |
| Trigonellin | **3–6 mg** | Keine etablierte Referenz |
| Koffein (Kaffee) | 29–34 mg | — |
| Koffein (gesamt mit Zusatz) | **130–185 mg** | 150–200 mg Pre-Workout optimal |
