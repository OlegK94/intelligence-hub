# Longevity Stack Ingredients

## Overview

This page documents the full ingredient framework for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] longevity coffee stack, organized by tier based on regulatory risk and commercial readiness.

## Tier-1 — Morning Performance (Regulatory-Safe)

These ingredients are commercially available, have no EU Novel Food barriers, and form the **MVP stack**.

| Ingredient | Category | Primary Role | Regulatory Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coffee (Arabica/Robusta)** | Base | Caffeine + [[Chlorogenic Acid]] + [[Trigonellin]] | ✅ Standard food |
| **[[L-Theanin]]** | Amino acid | Smooth focus, reduces caffeine jitter | ✅ Clear |
| **[[Alpha-GPC]]** | Choline | Acetylcholine / cognitive performance | ✅ Clear |
| **Lion's Mane** | Mushroom extract | Neurotrophin (NGF) support, cognitive | ✅ Clear (food history) |
| **Ashwagandha** | Adaptogen | Cortisol regulation, stress adaptation | ✅ Clear |
| **[[Creatine]]** | Amino acid derivative | Physical + cognitive performance | ✅ Clear |
| **Taurin** | Amino acid | Cardioprotective, emerging longevity data | ✅ Clear |

### Coffee Base Compounds (Naturally Occurring)

The coffee base itself delivers longevity-relevant compounds:
- **[[Chlorogenic Acid]]** — AMPK activation, insulin sensitivity
- **[[Trigonellin]]** — NAD+ precursor pathway, neuroprotection
- **Caffeine** — Performance, fat oxidation, Parkinson's risk reduction

## Tier-2 — Longevity Pro SKU (Pending Regulatory Clarity)

These ingredients have higher longevity potency but face regulatory or IP barriers in the EU.

| Ingredient | Category | Primary Role | Blocker |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[[NMN]]** | NAD+ precursor | Direct NAD+ elevation, mitochondrial health | 🔴 EU Novel Food status unclear |
| **[[Urolithin A]]** | Mitophagy inducer | Mitochondrial quality control, muscle health | 🔴 [[Amazentis]] patent + Novel Food |
| **Spermidine** | Polyamine | Autophagy induction, anti-aging | 🟡 DE Novel Food status unclear |

## NAD+ Pathway Strategy

The product's NAD+ narrative depends on regulatory outcome:

```
If NMN approved:
  → Direct NAD+ precursor claim (NMN in Tier-2)
  
If NMN NOT approved:
  → Trigonelline as natural NAD+ activator (in Tier-1 coffee base)
  → "Our coffee naturally contains a NAD+ pathway activator"
  → Scientifically defensible, no Novel Food burden
```

## Formulation Challenges

| Challenge | Detail |
|---|---|
| NAC odor masking | NAC has a sulfurous smell — must be masked in instant sachet |
| Curcumin solubility | Poor water solubility — requires liposomal or other enhanced form |
| Instant sachet compatibility | All ingredients must dissolve cleanly in hot water |
