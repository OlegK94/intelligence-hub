# Schutzgas-Versiegelung (Protective Gas Sealing)

## Overview

**Schutzgas-Versiegelung** (protective gas sealing) refers to the process of sealing sachets or packaging under a **nitrogen (N₂) atmosphere** — replacing the oxygen-containing air inside the package with inert nitrogen gas. This is a critical manufacturing requirement for [[Performance Coffee Brand]] sachets containing oxidation-sensitive longevity ingredients.

---

## Why Required

### Ingredient Sensitivity

| Ingredient | Oxidation Risk | Without N₂ Sealing |
|---|---|---|
| NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) | HIGH | Degrades rapidly in presence of O₂ and moisture |
| NR (Nicotinamide Riboside) | HIGH | Similar oxidation pathway to NMN |
| Astaxanthin | HIGH | Powerful antioxidant that itself oxidizes |
| CoQ10 (Ubiquinol form) | MEDIUM-HIGH | Ubiquinol oxidizes to ubiquinone, losing bioavailability advantage |
| Lion's Mane extract | LOW-MEDIUM | Shelf-life impact; less critical than NMN/NR |

**Without N₂ sealing:** NMN and NR can degrade significantly over the product's intended 18-month shelf life — potentially rendering the product ineffective or mislabeled (stated vs. actual content).

### Regulatory Implication

The mandatory **HPLC ingredient content testing** (one of 5 pre-launch tests per [[Performance Cafe Supplier List]]) must show ingredient content within specification at the labeled date. If NMN/NR degrades without N₂ sealing, the product would fail HPLC testing at shelf-life endpoints.

---

## Technical Requirements

### The Sealing Process

1. **Fill:** Sachet filled with powder mix
2. **Flush:** Oxygen flushed out with N₂ gas (nitrogen purge)
3. **Seal:** Sachet heat-sealed under N₂ atmosphere
4. **Result:** Headspace O₂ typically <0.5% (vs. 21% in ambient air)

### Packaging Compatibility

**Aluminium-Verbundfolie** (aluminium composite foil from [[Constantia Flexibles]]) is the correct packaging material for N₂ sealing:
- Near-zero oxygen transmission rate (OTR)
- Near-zero moisture vapor transmission rate (MVTR)
- Creates a hermetic barrier that maintains the N₂ atmosphere for 18+ months

Paper-based or standard plastic sachets cannot maintain N₂ atmosphere effectively — aluminium is required.
