# Chlorogenic Acid

## Overview

**Chlorogenic acid (CGA)** is the primary polyphenol in coffee and one of the most abundant dietary polyphenols. It is a key contributor to coffee's health benefits beyond caffeine, with established mechanisms in metabolic health and emerging longevity research.

## Mechanisms of Action

| Mechanism | Effect |
|---|---|
| **AMPK activation** | Activates AMP-activated protein kinase — the cellular energy sensor linked to longevity pathways |
| **Insulin sensitivity** | Reduces glucose absorption, improves insulin signaling |
| **Antioxidant** | Scavenges free radicals, reduces oxidative stress |
| **Anti-inflammatory** | Modulates inflammatory cytokines |
| **Gut microbiome** | Modulates gut bacteria composition |

### AMPK Connection
AMPK is activated by:
- Exercise
- Caloric restriction
- Metformin
- **Chlorogenic acid**

AMPK activation is associated with improved metabolic health, mitochondrial biogenesis, and autophagy — core longevity mechanisms. This places chlorogenic acid in the same pathway as some of the most researched longevity interventions.

## Coffee Source Optimization

### Roast Impact

| Roast Level | Chlorogenic Acid | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Light** | ✅ Maximum (~8–12% DW) | Preferred for longevity |
| **Medium** | Moderate (~4–7% DW) | Balanced approach |
| **Dark** | ❌ Low (<2% DW) | CGA degraded by heat |

**Light roast is the clear choice** for maximizing chlorogenic acid — and it also maximizes [[Trigonellin]].

### Vietnamese Robusta Advantage

Robusta coffee (Coffea canephora) contains significantly **more chlorogenic acid** than Arabica:
- Robusta: ~7–10% chlorogenic acids (dry weight)
- Arabica: ~5–7% chlorogenic acids (dry weight)

Vietnamese Robusta is specifically cited as the highest-CGA coffee source for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] base.

## Strategic Role in Performance Coffee Brand

Chlorogenic acid supports the product narrative at multiple levels:
1. **Performance:** AMPK activation supports metabolic efficiency during training
2. **Longevity:** AMPK is a master longevity regulator
3. **Metabolic health:** Insulin sensitivity supports body composition goals
4. **Differentiation:** Most coffee brands ignore CGA content; optimizing for it is a defensible claim
