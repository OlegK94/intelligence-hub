# Zone 2 Cardio

## Overview

Zone 2 cardio refers to aerobic exercise performed at a moderate intensity (typically 60-70% max heart rate) where the body primarily burns fat for fuel and can maintain conversation.

## Protocol Implementation

In [[Health Protocol Master]]:
- **Day:** Sunday
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Activity:** Walking with dog
- **Purpose:** Active recovery and fat oxidation

## Physiological Benefits

- Enhanced fat oxidation capacity
- Improved aerobic base and endurance
- Mitochondrial density increase
- Active recovery from intense training
- Cardiovascular health improvement

## Training Characteristics

- **Intensity:** Conversational pace
- **Heart Rate:** 60-70% of maximum
- **Fuel Source:** Primarily fat oxidation
- **Duration:** Extended sessions (45-90 minutes)
- **Frequency:** 1-3x per week depending on training goals

## Integration Strategy

- Complements [[HIIT Training]] for comprehensive cardiovascular development
- Serves as active recovery day in 6-day training split
- Combined with functional activity (dog walking)
- Low stress on joints and nervous system
