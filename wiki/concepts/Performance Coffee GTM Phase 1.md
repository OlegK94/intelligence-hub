# Performance Coffee GTM Phase 1

## Überblick

**Phase 1** ist der Operative Launch-Horizont für die [[Performance Coffee Brand]], der sich über 6 Monate (Wochen 11–24 nach Auftragsstart) erstreckt und vom Soft-Launch (Friends & Family) bis zur stabilen Skalierung (500+ Orders/Monat) führt. Er kombiniert Shopify als primärer Kanal mit sekundären Kanälen (Amazon, Influencer-Seeding) und etabliert die operativen Fundamente (Fulfillment, Customer Support, KPI-Tracking).

> Für den globalen Playbook-Kontext siehe [[Performance Coffee DTC Playbook]].
> Für langfristige Phasen (Phase 2+) siehe [[07 Vermarktung & Operations (Quelldokument)]].

---

## Phase 1 Timeline

### Soft Launch (Wochen 11–12)

**Ziel:** 50 Orders, Friends & Family validation, CAC-free

- **Go-Live:** Minimal viable Shopify Store
- **Audience:** Bestehende Community (Olegs Newsletter, Instagram, persönliche Netzwerk)
- **Incentive:** Early-Bird 20% Discount (limited to 50 orders)
- **Metriken:** 50 Orders, €2.500 Revenue, Repeat Rate ≥10%

### Public Launch (Wochen 13–16)

**Ziel:** 200 Orders/Monat, €10k Revenue, CAC <€28

- **Channels:** Meta Ads (Lookalike), Micro-Influencer Seeding, Owned-Channel Content
- **Shopify Optimization:** Cart Recovery, Email Drip Campaigns, Product-Page A/B Testing
- **Content Ramp:** 3× Instagram Reels/Woche, 1× Newsletter/Woche, YouTube Shorts
- **Metriken:** 200 Orders, €10k Revenue, 20% Repeat Rate, CAC <€28

### Scale & Optimization (Wochen 17–24)

**Ziel:** 500 Orders/Monat, €25k Revenue, CAC €22

- **Amazon DE Launch:** Week 19, Listing Optimization, Ads setup
- **Podcast Strategy:** 2–3 host-read placements (~€2–3k each)
- **Athlete UGC Campaign:** 10 micro-athletes, monthly giveaway
- **Abo-Cohort Analysis:** LTV-by-Channel tracking, Retention-Curve optimization
- **Metriken:** 500 Orders, €25k Revenue, 25% Repeat Rate, CAC €22, 45% Abo-Anteil

## Shopify DE — Channel Specification

### Store Setup

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Domain** | aevum.de (oder aevum.com mit DE-Locale) |
| **Platform** | Shopify Plus oder Standard (via Wagglz GmbH Infrastruktur) |
| **Theme** | Custom (nicht Standard Shopify theme) — Performance + Brand aesthetics |
| **Language** | German (primary), English (secondary option) |
| **Payment Gateways** | Shopify Payments (primary), PayPal, Klarna |
| **Shipping Zones** | DACH (primary), EU (secondary) |
| **Currency** | EUR (€) for all |

### Core Pages

- Homepage: Aevum brand narrative + "Perform now. Live longer" positioning
- Product Page (Sachets): Stack-A details, Koffein-Gehalt, MHD, FAQ
- Subscription Hub: Abo-optionen (Monthly/Annual), Flex-Pause, Flavor-Switch
- Blog/Resources: Protocols, Science, Recipes (SEO + Content Flywheel)
- About: Founder story (Oleg), Production story (CM + Kaffee-Sourcing)
- FAQ: Dosage, Storage, Shipping, Returns
- Contact: Email + Chat (Gorgias or Shopify Inbox)

### Checkout Flow

1. **Pre-Checkout:** Cart with Discount-Code field (visible early)
2. **Subscription Option:** Toggle "Subscribe & Save 15–20%" before cart
3. **Payment:** Shopify Payments (fastest), PayPal, Klarna (3 payments)
4. **Post-Purchase:** Instant confirmation email + Tracking link

## Subscription Model

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | SKU | Frequency | Price | Discount | LTV-Estimate (12m) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **One-Time Starter** | Single Box | 1× | €59 | — | €59 |
| **Monthly Flex** | 250g + 30 Sachets | Every 30d | €44 | 15% off €51 | €528 |
| **Monthly Performance** | 250g + 60 Sachets | Every 30d | €49 | 20% off €61 | €588 |
| **Annual Power** | 12× Monthly boxes | 12× | €39/mo (€468/yr) | 25% off €576 | €468 |

**Hinweis:** Annual-Tier ist LTV-optimal; Upsell-Strategie: Start Monthly, upgrade to Annual nach 3 Monaten.

### Abo-Mechaniken (via Shopify Subscriptions oder Recharge)

| Funktion | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Pause/Skip** | 2× per year, no penalty |
| **Frequency Change** | Anytime (biweekly optional) |
| **Flavor-Swap** | Power/Focus blends (same price tier) |
| **Cancellation** | Self-service, but trigger "save" offer (€5 off next order) |
| **Renewal Incentive** | 5 free sachets at 12-month renewal |

## Fulfillment Strategy

### Phase 1A–B: Self-Fulfillment (<100 Orders/Month)

**Setup:** Oleg or Hai's office/garage

| Step | Owner | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| **Pick** | Receive order notification (email) | Within 2 hours |
| **Pack** | Sachets + Branded Box + Thank-You Card | Within same day |
| **Label & Ship** | DHL/DPAG pickup | Within 24 hours |
| **Tracking** | Email customer with tracking link | Within 1 hour of ship |

**Cost Estimate:** €3–5 per order (boxes, labels, time)

### Phase 1C: 3PL Transition (>100 Orders/Month)

**Trigger:** Month 3 (200 Orders/Month projected)

**Provider Options (DE):**
- Fulfillment Box (Berlin) — Performance-Coffee-friendly
- DHL Fulfillment (Hamburg) — Expensive but reliable
- Sendcloud with 3PL partner — Hybrid cloud + warehouse

**Cost:** €4–7 per order (Pick-Pack-Ship)

**Setup:** Inventory sync via Shopify app; weekly reconciliation

## Customer Support

### Channels & SLAs

| Channel | SLA | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| **Email (support@aevum.de)** | 24h Werktag | Hai (primary) |
| **Instagram DM** | 48h | Oleg (spot-check) |
| **Shopify Chat** | 4h (9am–5pm CET) | Hai or Oleg |

### Common Queries (FAQ)

1. **Dosage:** "Wie viel Koffein pro Sachet?" → Label direkt (ca. 150 mg)
2. **Storage:** "Wie lange lagern?" → Cool, dry place; 18 months from production
3. **Allergies:** "Enthält Gluten/Nüsse?" → Label review (allergen matrix)
4. **Abo-Cancel:** "Wie cancele ich?" → Self-service in account, or email support
5. **Shipping:** "Wann kommt meine Bestellung?" → Tracking link in confirmation email
6. **Returns:** "Nicht zufrieden?" → 30-day money-back guarantee (full refund)

## Content Strategy & Owned-Channel Building

### Instagram

- **Frequency:** 3× Reels per week + 2× Stories per day
- **Content Mix:** Founder (Oleg) morning protocols (40%), Athlete features (30%), Science explainers (20%), UGC (10%)
- **Engagement Goal:** 5–10% engagement rate

### Newsletter (Substack)

- **Frequency:** 1× per week (every Tuesday)
- **Content Mix:** Longevity protocol deep-dive (40%), Product updates (20%), Reader Q&A (20%), Science news (20%)
- **Subscriber Goal:** 500+ by Month 6 (seeded from Shopify orders + landing page)

### YouTube Shorts

- **Frequency:** 2× per week
- **Content:** 60-second brewing tips, taste test clips, Oleg morning routine highlights
- **Goal:** 10k views/month by Month 6

### Blog (Shopify)

- **Frequency:** 1× per week
- **Topics:** "L-Theanin Dosage Guide", "Functional Coffee vs. Regular Coffee", "Koffein & Sleep", "Protocol Engineering"
- **SEO Goal:** Rank for "functional coffee DACH", "nootropic coffee", etc.

## Risiken & Mitigations

| Risiko | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Health-Claims-Compliance** | Audit alle Claims vor Launch; nur EFSA-approved formulieren |
| **High CAC (>€35)** | Pause Paid nach 1 Monat wenn CAC >€35; doppel-down auf Owned |
| **Low Repeat Rate (<15%)** | Increase Abo-Rabatt zu 20–25%; Flavor-Rotation einführen |
| **Inventory Overstock** | Pre-order model statt full-batch für <200 Orders/Monat |
| **Fulfillment Delays** | 3PL fallback plan (bereit für >100 Orders/Monat transition) |
