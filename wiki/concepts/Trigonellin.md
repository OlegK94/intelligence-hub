# Trigonellin (Trigonelline)

## Overview

**Trigonelline** (also written Trigonellin) is an alkaloid naturally occurring in coffee beans, alongside caffeine and [[Chlorogenic Acid]]. It is formed from nicotinic acid (niacin/Vitamin B3) and serves as one of coffee's key bioactive compounds beyond caffeine.

## Mechanisms of Action

| Mechanism | Effect |
|---|---|
| **NAD+ precursor pathway** | Trigonelline is metabolized to nicotinic acid (niacin), a precursor to NAD+ — linking coffee consumption to NAD+ biosynthesis |
| **Neuroprotection** | Shown to protect neurons from oxidative stress and promote neuronal growth |
| **Glucose metabolism** | Improves insulin sensitivity and glucose tolerance |
| **Antioxidant** | Direct free-radical scavenging activity |

## Coffee Source Characteristics

### Roast Impact

| Roast | Trigonelline Level | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Light** | ✅ Maximum | Preserved by low heat |
| **Medium** | Moderate | Partial degradation |
| **Dark** | ❌ Minimal | Converted to pyridines (bitter compounds) during roasting |

Light roast simultaneously maximizes both Trigonelline and [[Chlorogenic Acid]] — supporting the choice of a light-medium roast base for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]].

## Strategic Role — NMN Fallback

If [[NMN]] is not authorized as EU Novel Food:
- Trigonelline becomes the **primary NAD+ narrative anchor**
- It is **naturally occurring in coffee** — no Novel Food classification needed
- It is **not patented** in the context of coffee consumption
- The story: *"Our coffee base itself contains a natural NAD+ precursor — you don't need synthetic NMN"*

This makes Trigonelline a strategically important "free" differentiation asset that is intrinsic to the product, not an added ingredient.

## Role in Product Narrative

The Performance Coffee Brand can use Trigonelline to:
1. **Differentiate** from generic instant coffee (which uses dark roast, destroying Trigonelline)
2. **Support longevity claims** without Novel Food regulatory burden
3. **Bridge the gap** between Tier-1 (regulatory-safe) and Tier-2 (pending) stack narratives
