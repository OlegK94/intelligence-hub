# Sleep Optimization

## Overview

Sleep optimization is a core component of [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]]'s [[Health Protocol Master]]. It encompasses both **hardware** (mattress, pillow) and **biochemical** interventions (supplements) to maximize sleep quality and recovery.

## Hardware: Mattress & Pillow

### Mattress (Decision: Concluded)

See [[TEMPUR Matratzenkauf]] for full detail.

- **Product:** 2× TEMPUR PRO Plus SmartCool, 90×200 cm
- **Oleg:** Medium Firm (~100 kg, Rücken + Seite)
- **Partnerin:** Medium (~60 kg)
- **Rationale:** 40 kg weight difference → separate firmness levels optimal for both
- **SmartCool over CoolQuilt:** Washable cover + lower price; identical core

### Pillows

- **Oleg (188 cm):** TEMPUR Shape SmartCool Queen L
- **Partnerin:** TEMPUR Shape SmartCool Queen M
- **Relevance:** Proper pillow height supports cervical alignment — directly relevant to [[Forward Head Posture]] correction protocol (30° deviation)

## Posture During Sleep

Sleep posture is a passive corrective opportunity for [[Forward Head Posture]]:
- Proper pillow height (L size for Oleg at 188 cm) prevents cervical hyperextension or flexion during sleep
- Back sleeping and side sleeping positions require different pillow loft — the Medium Firm mattress supports both positions for Oleg
- Addresses the 30° FHP identified at [[3D Body Scan Scaneca Mai 2026]]

## Circadian and Environmental Factors

From [[Andrew Huberman]] protocols referenced in [[Health Protocol Master]]:
- Morning light exposure for circadian rhythm regulation
- Temperature regulation for sleep onset
- [[NSDR]] (Non-Sleep Deep Rest) as supplementary recovery tool
