# Kölner Liste

## Overview

The **Kölner Liste** is a certification program for sports supplements and food products, focused on verifying the **absence of substances prohibited in competitive sport** (WADA prohibited list). It is administered by the Centre for Preventive Doping Research at Deutsche Sporthochschule Köln.

## Why It Matters for Performance Coffee Brand

- **Target audience:** Performance athletes (Hyrox, CrossFit, competitive sport)
- **Standard expectation:** Serious athletes and sports retailers require Kölner Liste or equivalent certification
- **Credibility:** Signals product purity and quality commitment
- **DACH market standard:** The baseline certification for sports supplement sales in Germany, Austria, Switzerland

## Certification Details

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Cost | ~1.500–2.500 € per product |
| Prerequisite | Fixed CMO + finalized recipe |
| Scope | Absence of WADA-prohibited substances + heavy metals + microbiology |
| Renewal | Per batch (or periodic per agreement) |

## Relationship to Other Certifications

| Certification | Scope | When Needed |

| **Informed Sport** | Pan-European, more rigorous | EU Scale Phase |
