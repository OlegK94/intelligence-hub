# Wagglz GmbH

## Overview

**Wagglz GmbH** is a GmbH (Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung) for which [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg Kober]] serves as both Gesellschafter (shareholder) and Geschäftsführer (managing director / GF).

It is one of two company entities referenced in [[Oleg Financial Situation]], alongside [[OK Capital UG]].

## Capital Origin

The company was funded via a notable capital chain:
```
VW-Bank-Kredit → Crypto → Wagglz GmbH
```
This means the VW-Bank Kredit (681,57 €/month, included in Oleg's personal fixed expenses) was the **original capital source** that flowed through crypto investments into Wagglz GmbH. The credit obligation remains on Oleg's personal balance sheet.
