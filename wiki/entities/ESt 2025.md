# Einkommensteuer 2025 (ESt 2025)

## Overview

**ESt 2025** refers to [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]]'s Einkommensteuererklärung for the 2025 tax year. The filing is **mandatory** (Pflichtveranlagung) due to ALG I receipt exceeding the §46 EStG threshold, and is being prepared by [[SP STB]] (Steuerberater).

> For the primary source document, see [[ESt 2025 Source Detail]].

**Geschätztes zvE 2025:** ~30.000–35.000 €

## Statutory Filing Basis

**§46 EStG Pflichtveranlagung:** Filing is mandatory because ALG I (~8.400 €) exceeds the statutory threshold of 410 €/year for Lohnersatzleistungen.

## ALG I Progressionsvorbehalt

The ~8.400 € ALG I is tax-free income but triggers §32b EStG:
- ALG I added fictionally to taxable income to determine applicable tax rate
- Higher rate applied to actual taxable income (GF salary)
- Estimated additional burden: ~2.000 € (see [[ALG I Progressionsvorbehalt]])
