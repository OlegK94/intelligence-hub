# Chromadex

## Overview

**ChromaDex** is a US-based biotech company known for developing and patenting **Nicotinamide Riboside (NR)**, sold under the brand name **Tru Niagen**. Its patent portfolio creates overlapping IP risk for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]]'s use of [[NMN]].

## Relevance to Performance Coffee Brand

### Patent Landscape

ChromaDex holds patents related to:
- Nicotinamide Riboside (NR) as a supplement ingredient
- NAD+ precursor applications
- Potentially overlapping claims with NMN depending on synthesis method and sourcing

**Key risk:** Some NMN manufacturers may rely on synthesis processes that touch ChromaDex-adjacent IP. The [[Performance Coffee Brand]] must audit NMN supplier patents as part of the IP landscape research.

### NR vs. NMN

| Compound | ChromaDex Position | Status |
|---|---|---|
| NR (Nicotinamide Riboside) | ✅ Core product (Tru Niagen) | Patented, commercialized |
| NMN (Nicotinamide Mononucleotide) | ⚠️ Overlapping IP possible | Must be researched |

NMN converts to NR in the body as part of the NAD+ biosynthesis pathway — this metabolic relationship creates potential IP complexity even for NMN products.

## Key Research Required

From `legal/ip_landscape.md` (Priority 2):
- Full ChromaDex patent audit for NMN relevance
- Identify NMN suppliers with clean IP (no ChromaDex entanglement)
- Assess NR as alternative to NMN if NMN IP landscape is too complex

## Context: ChromaDex vs. Elysium Health

ChromaDex was involved in significant IP litigation with Elysium Health (a competing NR supplement company), which illustrates the aggressiveness of their IP enforcement approach. This increases risk for any NMN/NR-adjacent product.
