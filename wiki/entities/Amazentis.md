# Amazentis

## Overview

**Amazentis** is a Swiss clinical-stage biotech company that developed and commercialized **Urolithin A** under the brand name **MITOPURE**. It is one of the two critical IP blockers (alongside [[Chromadex]]) for the [[Performance Coffee Brand]]'s longevity ingredient stack.

## Relevance to Performance Coffee Brand

### Patent Holdings

Amazentis holds patents on [[Urolithin A]] covering:
- The use of Urolithin A as a supplement/food ingredient
- Specific synthesis/extraction processes
- Health claim applications

Any use of Urolithin A in the [[Performance Coffee Brand]] product may require:
1. **Licensing from Amazentis** (cost and terms unknown — must be negotiated)
2. **Alternative form** not covered by existing patents
3. **Waiting for patent expiry** (unlikely on a commercial timeline)

### Novel Food Authorization

Amazentis obtained **EU Novel Food authorization** for Urolithin A as MITOPURE:
- Authorization is **proprietary** — initially only Amazentis can commercially use it in the EU
- Proprietary period: typically 5 years from authorization grant
- After expiry: authorization becomes generic (any company may use)

**This creates a dual barrier:** both patent licensing AND Novel Food authorization compliance are required.
