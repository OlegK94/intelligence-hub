## Tax Implications for ESt 2025

ALG I is **steuerfreie Einnahme** (tax-exempt income) in Germany. However, it is subject to the **Progressionsvorbehalt** (§ 32b EStG):

- ALG I itself is not taxed
- But it is added to total income for the purpose of **calculating the applicable tax rate**
- The resulting higher rate is then applied to all **taxable** income (e.g., the [[Wagglz GmbH]] GF salary)
- Effect: The GF salary is taxed at a higher marginal rate than it would be without the ALG I

### Statutory References

- **§32b EStG Progressionsvorbehalt:** erhöht den Steuersatz auf das übrige Einkommen
- **§46 EStG:** Pflichtveranlagung wenn Lohnersatzleistungen > 410 €/Jahr — at ~8.400 €, this threshold is clearly exceeded

This is a material consideration for [[ESt 2025]] and [[SP STB]] should ensure correct Progressionsvorbehalt calculation.
