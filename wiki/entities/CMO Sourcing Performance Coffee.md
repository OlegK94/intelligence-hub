# CMO Sourcing — Performance Coffee Brand

## Overview

**CMO (Contract Manufacturing Organization)** sourcing is one of the core operational workstreams for [[Performance Coffee Brand]]. The CMO will manufacture the Instant-Kaffee-Sachet product — combining coffee base with the functional ingredient stack — at pilot scale (1,000–2,000 sachets) and later at commercial scale (10,000–50,000 sachets/month).

## CMO Selection Criteria

### Must-Have
1. **GMP Certification** — prerequisite for [[Kölner Liste]] (Phase 1 certification target)
2. **Instant coffee + functional ingredient co-formulation** capability
3. **Aluminium-Verbundfolie stick-pack** manufacturing capability
4. **MOQ compatibility** — pilot batch ≤ 2,000 sachets

### Strongly Preferred
5. **[[Kölner Liste]]-ready** — existing relationship or certification pipeline
6. **NAC odor masking** capability — flavorist or microencapsulation expertise
7. **Longevity/functional ingredient experience** (complex multi-ingredient stacks)
8. **German/DACH-based** — preferred for regulatory alignment with [[NEM Konformität]]

### Nice to Have
9. **Informed Sport** capability — for Phase 2 certification (see [[Certification Roadmap Performance Coffee Brand]])
10. **IFS Food** certified

## Key Technical Challenge: NAC Odor Masking

NAC (N-Acetyl-Cysteine) has a characteristic **sulfur odor** that could negatively impact product experience in a sachet format. Solutions:
- Flavorist-based masking
- Microencapsulation of NAC
- Alternative NAC forms

> ⚠️ **Contradiction Note:** NAC does not appear in the official Tier-1 stack defined in [[Performance Coffee Brand Hai Onepager]] or [[Performance Coffee Brand CLAUDE Project Context]]. Its presence in the CMO email suggests it may be a Tier-2 candidate or a legacy draft item. Stack definition documents should clarify.

## Questions Asked of CMO Candidates

1. Grundsätzliche Machbarkeit (Instant-Kaffee + Tier-1 stack in einem Sachet)?
2. MOQ und ungefähre Kosten für Pilot-Batch?
3. Zertifizierungen der Anlage? (GMP, ISO, Kölner-Liste-ready?)
4. Erfahrung mit NAC-Maskierung oder Flavoristen-Netzwerk?

## Relationship to Certification Roadmap

CMO selection is a **dependency** in [[Certification Roadmap Performance Coffee Brand]]:
- GMP-certified CMO → enables [[Kölner Liste]] application
- Informed Sport-approved CMO → enables Phase 2 certification without CMO switch
- CMO choice locks in Phase 1 certification path
