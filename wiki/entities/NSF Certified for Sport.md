# NSF Certified for Sport

## Overview

**NSF Certified for Sport** is the US gold standard for dietary supplements used by athletes. It is operated by **NSF International** and is the **Stufe 3** certification in the [[Performance Coffee Brand]]'s roadmap — reserved for the US expansion phase.

## What It Is

- The **most rigorous** supplement certification globally
- De facto **mandatory for professional athletes** in the USA (NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL all reference it)
- Recognized by major US sports organizations and anti-doping authorities
- Required for meaningful access to **Amazon US** listings in the sports nutrition category
- Required for entry into **US brick-and-mortar sports retail** (GNC, Vitamin Shoppe, etc.)

## What Makes It the Gold Standard

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Facility audit | NSF auditors inspect the manufacturing facility (CMO) on-site |
| Full traceability | Complete raw material traceability required |
| Every batch tested | All production batches independently tested |
| Label verification | Product label claims verified against tested content |
| Annual re-certification | Ongoing commitment, not one-time |
| International facility recognition | Non-US facilities (e.g., German CMO) must be individually approved by NSF |

## Special Challenge: German CMO Audit

For [[Performance Coffee Brand]], a critical complexity exists:
- The CMO is likely German-based (DACH production)
- **NSF must physically audit and approve the German manufacturing facility**
- This is not automatic — NSF does certify non-US facilities, but the process is longer and more expensive
- Must be factored into the US expansion timeline and budget

## Costs and Timeline

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Initial cost** | 5.000–10.000 € (includes facility audit) |
| **Annual re-certification** | Ongoing fee (not specified in source — typically 3,000–8,000 €/year) |
| **Timeline** | 4–6 months from application to certification |

## Trigger Condition

From the source: **only pursue at Phase 4 (concrete US expansion planning)**. This is the latest-stage certification with the highest investment — not appropriate until there is a validated US market entry strategy.

## Market Access It Unlocks

- **Amazon US** — sports nutrition category
- **US specialty retail** (GNC, Vitamin Shoppe, specialty gyms)
- **Professional sports partnerships** — prerequisite for sponsoring professional athletes in USA
- **D2C USA** — significantly increases consumer trust for US-based buyers
