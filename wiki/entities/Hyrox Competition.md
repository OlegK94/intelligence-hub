# Hyrox Competition

## Overview

**Hyrox** is a global indoor functional fitness race format that combines running with eight standardized workout stations. [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]] is an **active Hyrox competitor**. Preparation is one of the central performance projects within the [[Performance Overview]].

## Race Format

A standard Hyrox race consists of:
- **8 × 1km runs** alternating with
- **8 functional workout stations:**
  1. SkiErg — 1,000m
  2. Sled Push — 50m (102 kg men / 82 kg women)
  3. Sled Pull — 50m
  4. Burpee Broad Jump — 80m
  5. Rowing — 1,000m
  6. Farmers Carry — 200m
  7. Sandbag Lunges — 100m
  8. Wall Balls — 100 reps

## Preparation Strategy

The preparation framework is documented in [[Hyrox Prep Source Detail]] (12-week plan). The core strategic logic:

> **Zone 2 is the Hyrox foundation — do not skip it.**

### Three-Layer Training Structure

| Layer | Frequency | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| [[Zone 2 Training]] | 3–4x/week, 30–60 min | Aerobic base (primary limiter) |
| Kraft + Conditioning | 2x/week | Upper body hypertrophy + Hyrox stations |
| Haltungskorrektur | Daily, 10–15 min | Posture + breathing capacity |

### 12-Week Progression

| Phase | Weeks | Milestone |
|---|---|---|
| Fundament | 1–3 | 45 min Zone 2 without heart rate drift |
| Parallele Entwicklung | 4–6 | Sled Push/Pull weights increasing |
| Station Integration | 7–9 | Combination units (brick-style) |
| Race Simulation | 10–12 | Full Hyrox simulation |

See [[Brick Training]] for the methodology underlying the combination units in weeks 7–9.

## Key Protocols

### Cold Exposure Timing
Cold exposure (ice bath / cold shower) must NOT follow strength training — it blunts hypertrophic signaling. Allowed after Zone 2 sessions or on rest days.

### Zone 2 Intensity Gauge
- 60–70% HFmax
- Practical field test: nasal breathing test (can maintain nasal breathing throughout)
- Progression: +5 min/week once stable

### Corrective Work
- Chin Tucks + Dead Hangs + Thorax Extension daily
- Unilateral exercises preferred (addresses rightward asymmetry from scan)
- Direct effect: increased breathing capacity

## Structural Blockers (from Scan)

All three blockers are confirmed by [[3D Body Scan Scaneca Mai 2026]]:

1. **WHR 0.92** → visceral fat reduces diaphragm excursion and aerobic efficiency; target <0.86
2. **Forward Head Posture (30°)** → reduces thoracic volume and breathing capacity; corrected by daily posture protocol
3. **Bilateral asymmetry** (rightward CoG shift) → creates compensatory patterns under Hyrox load; addressed by unilateral training
