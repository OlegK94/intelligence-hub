## §536 BGB Vorbehalt

The **§536 BGB Vorbehalt** (reservation) notation in the rent booking indicates a **Mietminderungsvorbehalt** — a reservation of the right to reduce rent under §536 BGB (German Civil Code: rent reduction due to defects).

This means:
- Oleg (or GSW) has noted a **Mangel (defect)** in the rental property
- The full rent is being paid, but **under reservation** — preserving the legal right to claim a reduction retroactively
- This is a standard German tenant protection mechanism when a defect exists but is not yet resolved

> ⚠️ **Assumption [A]:** The specific nature of the defect (Mangel) is not known from this source. It could range from a minor issue to a significant structural problem. The Vorbehalt in the booking protects Oleg's claim without requiring immediate legal action.
