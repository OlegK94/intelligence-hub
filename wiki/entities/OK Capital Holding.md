## IP/OpCo Split Rationale

Separating IP from operations is a standard tax optimization strategy:
1. **IP held at holding** → IP income (license fees from OpCo) can be taxed at lower holding-company rate
2. **OpCo licenses IP from holding** → operating costs (license fees) are deductible at OpCo level
3. **Asset protection** → IP assets protected from operational liability
4. **Relocation-compatible** → consistent with [[Cyprus Relocation]] strategy; if Oleg moves to Cyprus, holding-level IP income may benefit from Cyprus's favorable IP regime
