# GROVE — Businessplan & Betriebshandbuch 2026/2027

## Overview

**GROVE** is a planned specialty coffee café with full bar, DJ events, and live-streaming scheduled to open in Berlin in February 2027. It operates through the existing [[Wagglz GmbH]] (to be renamed GROVE GmbH). The business plan (v3.3, June 2026) is the authoritative document for the venture.

> For the full source document detail, see [[GROVE Businessplan Source Detail]].
> For the original planning document with Hai, see [[Café Berlin Partnership Hai Raw Source]].

---

## The Concept

**"Ein Ort der sich wie eine zweite Wohnung anfühlt — von 8 Uhr morgens bis Mitternacht."**

- Daytime: Specialty Coffee + Backwaren
- Evening: Full Bar (Naturwein, Cocktails, Craftbier) + DJ Culture
- Always: Music, Community, Warmth
- Positioning gap: Berlin has great specialty cafés (Five Elephant, Bonanza, Acid) and good bars — almost no places combining both authentically with DJ community and no-club atmosphere

**Unique tagline:** *"Vietnamese Coffee. Japanese Chocolate. Berlin Music."*

> ⚠️ **Equity contradiction resolved:** Early planning documents (Café Berlin Partnership Hai) mentioned 50/50. This definitive business plan specifies **60/40**. The 60/40 split is authoritative.

> ⚠️ **Partner SV-Prüfung:** Als 40%-Gesellschafter ist Hai ggf. sozialversicherungspflichtig → AG-Kosten +~800 €/Monat ungeklärt.

## Location

**Primary targets:** Friedrichshain + Schöneberg
**Avoid:** Prenzlauer Berg objects with Ablöse > 30.000 € (PB Ablösen 85–135k sind common)

**Lease requirements:**
- Min. 5 Jahre + 5-Jahres-Verlängerungsoption
- 4–6 mietfreie Aufbaumonate
- Max. 40 €/qm (FHain) / 50 €/qm (PB)
- Schallschutzgutachten vor Mietvertragsunterzeichnung (DJ-Events)

## Pre-Opening Community Strategy

**[[GROVE Sessions External]]** — Start immediately, no café needed:
- Events in other Berlin specialty cafés (Five Elephant, Fjord, 19grams, smaller FHain/PB cafés)
- Pitch: "Wir bringen Community zu euch, ihr behaltet alle Getränkeeinnahmen"
- Revenue model: Ticket 10–15 € × 80–150 Gäste = 800–2.250 €/Event
- Timeline: First event August 2026, monthly rhythm from October 2026

Reference model: [[Espresso Club Radio Reference]] (Buenos Aires)

## Exit Options (Long-Term)

- **Strategischer Verkauf (Jahr 3–5):** 3–5× EBITDA or 0,8–1,2× Jahresumsatz; via Holding-GmbH → ~1,5% effective tax (§8b KStG)
- **Dividenden + Holdingstruktur:** 95% steuerfrei zwischen GmbH-Ebenen (§8b KStG)
- **Franchise/Lizenzierung (Jahr 4–6):** 5–8% Umsatz + 20–50k Einstiegsgebühr
