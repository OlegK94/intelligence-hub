# Performance Coffee Brand

## Overview

**Performance Coffee Brand** is [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg Kober]]'s functional beverage startup, co-developed with partner [[Hai]]. The product is an **Instant-Kaffee-Sachet** (8–12g stick-pack) combining a light roast instant coffee base with a scientifically dosed **Longevity + Performance** ingredient stack.

> The brand targets the unoccupied high-performance + high-longevity market quadrant — "the Blueprint coffee [[Bryan Johnson]] doesn't make."

## Product Definition

### Format
- **Type:** Instant coffee sachet (stick-pack)
- **Fill weight:** 8–12g
- **Packaging:** Aluminium-Verbundfolie, Schutzgas-Versiegelung
- **Pilot MOQ:** 1,000–2,000 sachets
- **Scale target:** 10,000–50,000 sachets/month

### Tier-1 Stack (NEM-konform)

| Ingredient | Role |
|---|---|
| Kaffee Light Roast Instant | Base — max [[Chlorogenic Acid]] + [[Trigonellin]] |
| [[L-Theanin]] | Calm focus synergy with caffeine |
| [[Alpha-GPC]] | Cognitive enhancement |
| Lion's Mane | Neurotrophin support |
| [[Ashwagandha KSM-66]] | Cortisol modulation |
| Kreatin Monohydrat | Strength + cognitive performance |
| [[Taurin]] | Mitochondrial + cardiovascular support |

> ⚠️ **NAC Note:** NAC appears in [[CMO Erstkontakt Email Template]] as a technical challenge item but is absent from the official Tier-1 stack. Possible Tier-2 candidate or legacy item — requires clarification.

## Market Positioning

See [[Competitive Landscape Performance Coffee]] for full matrix. Summary:
- **Blueprint** ([[Bryan Johnson]]): No coffee — creates the white space
- **Four Sigmatic:** Only 2 mushrooms, no longevity stack
- **RYZE:** 6 mushrooms, no real coffee/caffeine
- **MUD\WTR:** Anti-coffee positioning
- **Timeline Mitopure** ([[Amazentis]]): Single ingredient only

**Target price:** 3.49 €/portion

## Critical Blockers

### IP Blockers
- **[[Amazentis]]** — patents on [[Urolithin A]] + EU Novel Food authorization
- **[[Chromadex]]** — NMN/NR patent landscape

### Regulatory Blockers
- **[[EU Novel Food Regulation]]** — NMN and Urolithin A require clearance
- **[[NEM Konformität]]** — mandatory before any EU sale
- **EFSA Health Claims** — prevents "anti-aging" / longevity language on label
