# Performance Coffee Brand

## Overview

**Performance Coffee Brand** is [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]]'s **Priority 1** business project as of June 2026 — a functional, longevity-optimized instant coffee sachet brand targeting the intersection of performance (energy, focus, cognition) and longevity (mitochondrial health, autophagy, NAD+ metabolism, anti-inflammation).

**Core tagline:** *"Perform now. Live longer."*

**The white space:** [[Bryan Johnson]] removed coffee from his Blueprint protocol. His community still drinks coffee and wants a Blueprint-compatible product. Nobody has built that yet — this is the unoccupied top-right position on the Performance × Longevity matrix.

## Critical IP Blockers

Two major IP barriers affect the longevity ingredient stack:

### 1. Amazentis — Urolithin A
- **[[Amazentis]]** holds 50+ global patents + EU Novel Food authorization for [[Urolithin A]] (MITOPURE)
- Synthesis patent EP3087065B1 expires ~2034–2036
- **Recommended MVP approach:** Pomegranate extract (natural UA precursor) — no IP issues
- See [[Amazentis]] entity page for full analysis

### 2. Chromadex — NR (NIAGEN)
- **[[Chromadex / Niagen Bioscience]]** holds patents on Nicotinamide Riboside (NR/NIAGEN)
- Affects NR inclusion in EU
- Alternative: [[NMN]] (subject to its own EU regulatory status)
- See [[Chromadex / Niagen Bioscience]] entity page for full analysis

### 3. NMN — EU Regulatory Status
- [[NMN]] EU authorization status as of 2025 is uncertain and explicitly flagged as critical
- Must be resolved before NMN can be included in product

## Coffee Specification

(From Modul 2 brief — to be completed in `research/03_kaffee_specs.md`)

Key decision: **Vietnamese Robusta** (Buôn Ma Thuột, Dak Lak) as base — higher caffeine (up to 2.7%), higher chlorogenic acid content than Arabica. To be tested in 5 blend ratios (100/0 to 20/80 Robusta/Arabica).

Light roast preferred for **maximum chlorogenic acid and trigonellin retention** (both degrade significantly with dark roasting).
