## Supplier Profiles

### L-Theanin: Suntheanine (Taiyo)
- **Brand:** Suntheanine®
- **Manufacturer:** Taiyo International (Japan)
- **IP status:** Patented process; licensing is the business model (similar to KSM-66)
- **Regulatory:** GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) — US FDA
- **EU status:** Widely used in EU dietary supplements
- **Price:** 35–45 €/kg — accessible; Tier-1 stack compatible
- **Dose in stack:** 200mg per sachet
- **Cost per sachet:** ~0.007–0.009 € (negligible COGS impact)

### Alpha-GPC: Chemi Nutra
- **Manufacturer:** Chemi Nutra (US-based)
- **Quality:** USP-Grade (United States Pharmacopeia standard)
- **Price:** 110–130 €/kg — Medium-High tier
- **Dose in stack:** 150mg per sachet
- **Cost per sachet:** ~0.016–0.019 €
- **Note:** Alpha-GPC is hygroscopic (absorbs moisture) — relevant for sachet formulation stability; CMO must account for this

### Lion's Mane: Nammex
- **Manufacturer:** Nammex (North American Medicinal Mushroom Extracts, BC, Canada)
- **Product:** Dual Extract (water + ethanol extraction) — preserves both beta-glucans and hericenones
- **EU status:** EU-konform
- **Price:** 70–90 €/kg
- **Note:** "Dual extract" is the quality differentiator vs. mycelium-on-grain products; Nammex is the industry standard for verified fruiting-body Lion's Mane

### Ashwagandha: Ixoreal Biomed (KSM-66)
- **Brand:** KSM-66®
- **Manufacturer:** Ixoreal Biomed (India, proprietary root-only extraction)
- **IP:** Patented extraction process; actively licensed to brands (see [[Ashwagandha KSM-66]])
- **Clinical evidence:** 22+ gold-standard RCTs
- **Price:** 55–70 €/kg
- **Dose in stack:** 300mg per sachet

### Kreatin: Alzchem (Creapure)
- **Brand:** Creapure®
- **Manufacturer:** AlzChem Group AG (Germany — Bayern)
- **Differentiator:** Only German manufacturer of creatine monohydrate; highest purity standard; Informed Sport certified
- **Price:** 14–18 €/kg — lowest-cost Tier-1 ingredient
- **Dose in stack:** 3,000–5,000mg per sachet (if included)
- **Cost per sachet at 3g:** ~0.04–0.05 € — still very low

### Taurin: Taurine Pharma
- **Standard:** Food Grade
- **Price:** 10–14 €/kg — lowest-cost ingredient overall
- **Dose in stack:** 500–1,000mg
- **Note:** Taurin is a commodity ingredient; Taurine Pharma is a specialized supplier but alternatives exist

### NMN: Longevir / Effepharm
- **Dual-source recommendation** — single-source risk mitigation
- **Effepharm:** Chinese manufacturer with FSSC 22000 certification; major EU NMN supplier
- **Longevir:** EU-focused NMN supplier
- **Price:** 900–1,200 €/kg — Premium tier
- **Regulatory:** See [[NMN Regulatory Status]] — NMN is currently in a regulatory grey area in EU; not yet fully approved as Novel Food; this creates supply and compliance risk
- **Dose consideration:** 250–500mg per sachet → 0.225–0.600 € per sachet in ingredient cost alone — significant COGS impact

> ⚠️ **IP Note:** NMN is also subject to potential patent claims from Chromadex and others. See [[Performance Café IP Landscape Modul 8]] and [[Chromadex / Niagen Bioscience]] for details.

### Spermidine: TLL Pharma
- **Manufacturer:** TLL Pharma
- **Source:** Wheat germ extract (Weizenkeimextrakt) — natural food-derived source
- **Price:** 700–900 €/kg — Premium tier
- **Regulatory:** Wheat germ-derived spermidine is generally considered a food ingredient in EU; better regulatory position than synthetic spermidine
- **Dose consideration:** 1–5mg per sachet → very low COGS impact despite high price/kg (microdosing)

### NAC: BASF / Sigma
- **Manufacturers:** BASF (Germany — major chemical company) + Sigma-Aldrich
- **Standard:** Pharma-Grade
- **Price:** 25–35 €/kg — Low-Medium tier
- **Note:** NAC has an odor challenge (sulfur/thiol group) requiring flavorist masking — see [[Flavorist Services Performance Coffee]]
- **Stack status:** NAC not in confirmed Tier-1 stack — see contradiction note in [[Performance Cafe Supplier List]]

### Curcumin: Indena (Meriva)
- **Brand:** Meriva®
- **Manufacturer:** Indena SpA (Italy)
- **Technology:** Micellar / phytosome formulation — significantly improved bioavailability vs. standard curcumin
- **Price:** 130–160 €/kg — High tier
- **IP:** Meriva is a patented formulation; similar to KSM-66, Indena actively licenses it

### Astaxanthin: AstaReal / Cyanotech
- **Dual-source recommendation**
- **AstaReal:** Swedish company (algae cultivation in Iceland/Sweden); premium natural astaxanthin
- **Cyanotech:** Hawaii-based; BioAstin® branded natural astaxanthin from Haematococcus pluvialis algae
- **Source:** Both are natural (Haematococcus pluvialis algae) — vs. synthetic astaxanthin which is cheaper but considered inferior for human supplementation
- **Price:** 800–1,000 €/kg — Premium tier
- **Dose consideration:** 4–12mg per sachet → very low COGS despite high price/kg

### CoQ10: Kaneka
- **Manufacturer:** Kaneka Corporation (Japan)
- **Form:** Ubiquinol (reduced/active form) — superior bioavailability vs. ubiquinone
- **Quality:** Reinst (highest purity)
- **Price:** 350–450 €/kg — High tier
- **Note:** Kaneka is the world's largest producer of pharmaceutical-grade CoQ10; industry gold standard

## Coffee Base Suppliers

| Lieferant | Standort | Besonderheit |
|---|---|---|
| **Neumann Kaffee Gruppe** | Hamburg | Largest DE coffee commodity trader; direct import |
| **Sievert Storey** | Hamburg | Robusta Vietnam, direct Dak Lak contacts |
| **Coffee Circle B2B** | Berlin | Specialty coffee, direct producer relationships |
| **Trung Nguyen Export** | Vietnam (Dak Lak) | High-caffeine Robusta; direct import option |

**Strategic note on Robusta:** Vietnamese Robusta (Dak Lak origin) offers ~80–120mg caffeine per gram vs. ~40–70mg for Arabica. For a performance coffee brand targeting 100–150mg caffeine per sachet, Robusta base at smaller quantities achieves the dose target more cost-effectively.
