# Allianz Insurance Consolidation

## Overview

A financial optimization project for [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]] to consolidate insurance policies at Allianz, reaching the **5-policy threshold** that unlocks a **20% colleague discount** via an Allianz contact.

## Strategy

- **2 policies already at Allianz:** Zahnzusatz (dental supplement) + Auslandskrankenkasse/Reise (travel health)
- **3 policies to switch:** Privathaftpflicht + Hausrat + Rechtsschutz
- **1 policy NOT to switch:** Risikolebensversicherung (term life)

### Why Term Life Stays at Current Provider

The [[Risikolebensversicherung]] is explicitly excluded from consolidation:
- Switching requires a new **Gesundheitsprüfung** (health assessment) → risk of higher premiums
- New contract uses current (higher) entry age → permanently more expensive
- The 20% discount does **not** justify this permanent cost increase

See [[Allianz Insurance Consolidation Detail]] for full source documentation.
