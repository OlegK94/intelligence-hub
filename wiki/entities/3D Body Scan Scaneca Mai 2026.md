## Interpretation

- **KFA 20.3%:** Slight overweight, primarily lower abdomen
- **FFMI 21.3:** Solid muscle mass for a natural athlete
- **Lean Mass 77.1 kg:** Good base for a hypertrophy cycle
- **30° Forward Head:** Postural correction protocol indicated

## Target Values (Next Scan — ~Mid-September 2026)

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Body Fat | **15–17%** |
| Fat Mass | ~14 kg |
| Lean Mass | ≥77 kg (maintain) |
| Waist | <90 cm |

> ⚠️ **Note on KFA target:** The raw source specifies **15–17%** as the body fat target range. The [[Health Protocol Master]] source page states **15%** as the goal. The range (15–17%) is the operationally intended target from the scan document itself and is treated as authoritative here.

## Goals Set from This Baseline

- Reduce body fat from **20.3% → 15–17%** (focus: lower abdomen)
- Maintain or increase lean mass (≥77.1 kg)
- Reduce waist circumference to <90 cm
- Build upper chest and triceps hypertrophy
- Address 30° forward head posture through corrective protocol

## Use in Downstream Protocols

- **Nutrition:** 2,800 kcal daily target (845 kcal deficit from TDEE)
- **Protein:** 195–200g/day target based on lean mass
- **Training:** 6-day split designed around body composition goals
- **Posture:** Daily neck stretching + face pulls protocol
- **Follow-up scan:** ~12 weeks post-baseline (mid-September 2026)
