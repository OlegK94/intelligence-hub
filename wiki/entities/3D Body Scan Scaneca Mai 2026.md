## Interpretation

- **KFA 20.3%:** Slight overweight, primarily lower abdomen / visceral
- **FFMI 21.3:** Solid muscle mass for a natural athlete
- **Lean Mass 77.1 kg:** Good base for a hypertrophy cycle
- **30° Forward Head:** Postural correction protocol indicated; breathing capacity directly affected
- **WHR 0.92:** Above optimal range; visceral fat concern — target <0.86 via [[Zone 2 Training]] + nutrition
- **Bilateral asymmetry:** Rightward CoG shift — functional weakness + injury risk in Hyrox; corrective exercise priority

## Goals Set from This Baseline

- Reduce body fat from **20.3% → 15–17%** (focus: lower abdomen / visceral)
- Maintain or increase lean mass (≥77.1 kg)
- Reduce waist circumference to <90 cm
- Reduce WHR from 0.92 → <0.86 (via Zone 2 + nutrition)
- Build upper chest and triceps hypertrophy
- Address 30° forward head posture through corrective protocol
- Address bilateral asymmetry (rightward CoG shift)

## Use in Downstream Protocols

- **Nutrition:** 2,800 kcal daily target (~845 kcal deficit from TDEE)
- **Protein:** 195–200g/day (lean-mass-based) or ~220g/day (body-weight-based per [[Claude Session Template Performance]])
- **Training:** 6-day split designed around body composition goals
- **Posture:** Daily neck stretching + face pulls protocol
- **Zone 2:** Priority cardio modality for visceral fat (WHR reduction)
- **Follow-up scan:** ~12 weeks post-baseline; same lab, same time of day, fasted
