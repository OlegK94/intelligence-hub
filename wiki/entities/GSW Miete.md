## GSW Context

**GSW Immobilien** is now part of the **Deutsche Wohnen** / **Vonovia** group (Vonovia acquired Deutsche Wohnen, which had acquired GSW). It manages a large portfolio of Berlin apartments and is known for:
- Below-market regulated rents in many contracts
- Strong tenant protections under Berlin rental law
- Mixed portfolio across Berlin Bezirke

At **991,09 €/month**, the rent is **significantly below** current Berlin market rents for comparable apartments (typical 2–3 room apartments in Berlin run 1.200–2.000 €/month as of 2026). This suggests an older contract benefiting from Mietpreisbremse or Mietspiegel-regulated rates.

## Strategic Relevance

- This below-market rent is a **financial cushion** — it reduces the pressure on Oleg's net salary compared to market rent
- A key consideration for [[Cyprus Relocation]]: leaving this apartment means losing a valuable regulated Berlin tenancy that would be very difficult to replicate if Oleg returns to Berlin
- Relevant for [[Café Berlin Partnership Hai]]: if Oleg relocates but maintains the apartment, subletting may be an option under German tenancy law (requires landlord approval from GSW/Vonovia)
