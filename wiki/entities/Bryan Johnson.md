# Bryan Johnson

## Overview

Bryan Johnson is an entrepreneur and biohacker who has gained attention for his systematic approach to life extension and health optimization through his **Blueprint** protocol and product line (blueprint.bryanjohnson.com).

## Blueprint Program

### Philosophy
- **Science-backed, precision-dosed** — "every ingredient fought for its life, no pixie-dusting, no BS"
- Comprehensive health optimization system
- Data-driven approach to longevity
- Precise nutrition and exercise protocols
- Extensive biomarker tracking
- Sleep optimization strategies

### Current Blueprint Product Line (as of 2026)

Blueprint sells:
- Longevity Mix Pulver
- Ceremonial Matcha
- Super Shrooms
- Ashwagandha+Rhodiola
- NAC+Ginger+Curcumin
- Essential Capsules
- Creatine
- Omega-3
- Collagen

**Notably: Blueprint sells NO coffee.**

## Coffee Decision

Bryan Johnson **removed coffee from his personal protocol**. Despite this, his community continues to drink coffee and actively seeks a Blueprint-compatible coffee product. This creates the **white space** that [[Performance Coffee Brand]] targets:

> *"The Longevity Coffee Blueprint doesn't make."*

This is one of the clearest market opportunity signals in [[Oleg Personal Context|Oleg]]'s business concept.
