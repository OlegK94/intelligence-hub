# Aevum — Brand Name

## Overview

**Aevum** is the top-recommended brand name for [[Performance Coffee Brand]], selected from a field of 15 candidates in the [[Performance Coffee Brand Naming]] brief (Modul 7, 2026-06-14). It is a Latin word meaning *Lebenszeit* — the duration of a life, the lifespan — sitting between a single moment (*momentum*) and eternity (*aeternitas*).

---

## Etymology

| Language | Word | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Latin | *aevum* | Age, lifespan, era — the time of a life |
| Italian | *evo* (derived) | Age, era |
| French | *âge* (derived) | Age |
| Spanish | *edad* / *evo* (derived) | Age |
| English | *eon*, *age* (cognates) | Long period |

In medieval philosophy, *aevum* was distinct from *tempus* (time) and *aeternitas* (eternity) — it denoted the temporality of celestial beings and long-lived things. This gives the name both depth and accessibility.
