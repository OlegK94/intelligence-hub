## Design Principles

### Morning Anchor
- **05:00 wake** aligns with early training window before work
- Pre-gym supplementation during commute for optimal absorption timing
- Protein intake immediately post-workout during return commute

### Circadian Rhythm Support
- Coffee cut-off at 14:00 (6+ hours before sleep)
- Dim lights + screen night mode at 19:30
- [[NSDR]] session bridging dinner and sleep
- Phone removed from bedroom at 20:30

### Dog Walk Integration
- Three daily walks (morning, midday, evening)
- Sunday long walk = [[Zone 2 Cardio]] session
- Functional movement embedded into daily routine

### Sleep Target
- **8.5 hours** targeted (20:30–05:00)
- Sleep supplements at 20:25 for pre-sleep preparation
- NSDR at 20:00 as transition ritual

## Scientific Basis

The schedule incorporates principles from:
- [[Andrew Huberman]] — light exposure, caffeine timing, NSDR
- [[Rhonda Patrick]] — 3-hour pre-sleep fast (dinner at 19:30, sleep at 20:30)
- [[Bryan Johnson]] — systematic daily protocol adherence
