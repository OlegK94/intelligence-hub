## 3. Critical Risk Flags

### 🔴 Insolvency Risk (Both Companies)
- **§17 InsO** (Zahlungsunfähigkeit): inability to pay debts as they fall due
- **§19 InsO** (Überschuldung): liabilities exceed assets (equity negative)
- **Status:** Both companies require immediate document review (Jahresabschlüsse, BWA, Kontoauszüge, Gesellschaftsvertrag)
- Until documents are reviewed, insolvency cannot be ruled out for either entity

### 🔴 GF Liability (Double Exposure)
- Oleg is Geschäftsführer (GF) of **both** Wagglz GmbH and OK Capital UG
- **§17/19 InsO:** Once insolvency is established, GF has **21 days** to file (Antragspflicht)
- Failure → criminal liability for **Insolvenzverschleppung** (delayed insolvency filing)
- Personal liability risk is existential if insolvency is already technically present

### 🔴 Gesellschafterdarlehen Risk
- **§39 InsO:** Shareholder loans are subordinated in insolvency (paid last)
- **§135 InsO:** 10-year claw-back risk — repayments of shareholder loans within 10 years before insolvency can be reversed
- Current ~357 €/month Gesellschafterdarlehen payment creates ongoing exposure

### 🟡 Tax Status
- Steuererklärungen filing status unconfirmed
- Potential Verlustvorträge (loss carryforwards) unused — value unrealized
- Unresolved tax picture compounds overall risk
