# Rehabilitation Plan — 3-Phase Financial Recovery

## Executive Summary

**Rehabilitation Plan** is a structured 3-phase roadmap to resolve Oleg's structural annual deficit (€-7.970/year) and personal insolvency risk (€-1.405 dispo, €1.10 savings).

The plan is indexed to **two catalysts:**
1. **July Commission** (~€4.611 net) — immediate dispo recovery
2. **Performance Coffee Phase 1 Launch** (Q3/Q4 2026) — revenue inflection point

**Target Timeline:** Break structural deficit by **Month 12 (June 2027)** through combined cashflow improvements.

## Phase 2: Launch Preparation & Jahresabschluss Decision (August – September 2026)

### Diagnosis

By end of Phase 1:
- Dispo is cleared (no more overdraft fees)
- Corporate insolvency status is known
- ESt 2025 is filed (deadline passed, no penalties)
- Performance Coffee prototype is ordered (Week 1–2 August per Module 00 timeline)

### Actions

#### A. Jahresabschluss Priority Decision (by August 15)

**Problem:** Wagglz GmbH + OK Capital UG each require ~€5,000 Jahresabschluss (total €10,000/year). The July commission can partially cover, but full payment would eliminate all working capital.

**Option 1: Full Compliance (€10,000 paid immediately)**
- **Pros:** No legal risk; clean record; reduces stress
- **Cons:** Eliminates working capital; Performance Coffee funding is starved
- **Trigger:** Only if Performance Coffee is cancelled or heavily funded externally

**Option 2: Payment Plan with Accountant (€2,500 now, €2,500 in Q4)**
- **Pros:** Spread costs; preserve working capital for Performance Coffee launch
- **Cons:** Requires accountant negotiation; slight legal risk (but manageable if filed on time)
- **Trigger:** RECOMMENDED if Performance Coffee launch is on track

**Option 3: Wind-down Decision (File insolvency, liquidate entities)**
- **Pros:** Eliminates ongoing compliance burden; clarifies personal liability
- **Cons:** 6–12 month legal process; credit impact; potential personal liability clawback
- **Trigger:** Only if insolvency audit finds legal insolvency (unlikely for dormant entities)

**Recommendation:** **Option 2** — negotiate payment plan with SP STB; preserve Phase 1 DTC launch capital (~€5k–€10k for inventory, Shopify, ads).
